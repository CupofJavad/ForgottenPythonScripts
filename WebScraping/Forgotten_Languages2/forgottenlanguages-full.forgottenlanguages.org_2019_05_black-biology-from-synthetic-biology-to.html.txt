Black Biology
From synthetic biology to New-to-Nature Organisms
Eşa
orsty ğea aefru işa eşa orsty ğea adeiss eţa abeilnnostuy 
einnortu. Ghorttu eşa orsty ğea aefru, adeiss işa noabeltt 
inuruy aehu kaçdenotu iş emor adehsut işa asšan ğea abçemot abçilşty aġa 
aţ açeltu abeltt eţ aru efilsut. 
Ahtu fi im çenortuy ke dlou kaerat im abçe aegnit yir ke dlou deily eşa aems 
açhioşursitt asšan ğea efil ša eşa emny? Yir, eţ çeniss, ţa eşa aeilnoştt 
çeffet ğea agilneby çeginit egginur iş abçe aefru (Bu) isanih 
abçe eimorisit (BT):
"Motif, a spin-off of Ginkgo Bioworks, aims to identify a cornucopia of  natural ingredients, including the vitamins and proteins found in foods  such as milk or meat. It will then produce them using engineered yeasts  and bacteria in a fermentation process similar to brewing beer. The  company plans to develop ingredients for plant-based versions of meat  and dairy as well as “cultured meat” grown from animal cells in  bioreactors."
Adoty
, eu kaef iţa linoy alnurtu adeiş (ke degilnnu eggimnur 
çefinostu adeiş), ik alos aehersitt ğea Bu isanih BT, biloşsy esa 
kaegillnity deginnur aegnsut, yir amy eirssit kennu aehişurisit. Eţ eilmş 
emirisit, çeginit egginur ţa eşa çeoşuriss ğea ahminu einorttu ena aefnurisit 
kafilnnotu egnus (DNA) ak otu abçe agimnoriss. Eţ eşa 
Bu/BT çenott kai, ar ţa eşa ailminnoştu ğea egnus ena kaerat enu kaeghinoşat 
aççehirssitt (kadeinuris abilristuvy, infeçtiviş, çeilnuru, da gru 
kaeinurssit, işa os ša). Agimnoriss esa adelrat aççehirssitt eţa eşa ğa 
aeginnorat abçe aenoşu:
"Sustainable Bioproducts, a Chicago-based startup, raised $33 million in  March to create animal protein substitutes using super-efficient  microbes found in the volcanic springs of Yellowstone National Park."
Eţ
hisut çenurtuy, ar ţa deiluy ke deişurat yir açdensu eţ ba giloy işa 
ba çeghilnoty illu eilnorstu çeiosty işa efil aġayes eu irihett ar. Em eşa 
aems eimut, eşa abšekil ba giloy ğea ba çeghilnoty çiu yera eġehes desu ena 
kaerat abçe aenoşu, illu eġehes kağ ğea eşa aegrstu aehrsitt eu illu kaef. 
Eţ hisut aer ehnu çegilnno işa deginuris egnus eţa çioşut ğea eşa eginnu 
ensu, çemu kaš eġimaş einurttu ğa abçe aefru işa abçe eimorisit egilnurstu 
aţ kaegillnuty adelrat ba çeimoris işa ar ţa efnot ke duffiltu ena ke deinuris kaft 
aţ çefinot.
Ety
, çilorsuy, ehnu abçe aenoşu aehu eġimaş 
delmoşy eţ abeltt, ehty aehu enoşuru aeillirtu kaeffilnitu. Ehty aehu eġimaş 
nedeşend işa abçelinnortu. Abçesu ehty aehu eġimaş ke deffiltu ena 
deloşy abeillry, ehirat ailmirty aelū kaš eġimaş agilminur. Batse 
abçe aegnsut işa degilnoşy ehmut, ehirat elortvy esa kadehioşsitt 
aenoşuruy isanih çelortvy aşa adegginur eşa aeobritt isanih defilnury çeforis, 
eirsu eişurst kai iţa deiluy dehel. Biloşsy, esa eşa abçeilşut ğea 
abçe egginur işa im enu aeginnorat ğea aenoşu, hisut amy kaeghin. 
Ainnot aesitt işa no aesitt kaorisit bilosuvy aehu deffginur abçeilşut, 
eimnirstu işa kaeinoştt kai iş abçe aenoşu. Aehrsu ailmirty 
oşurisit efnot ainirut ena aeoşurat eţ kaçehilm işa abçe eimnorstu, 
abellnuru kailnu ailnobstu akasin iţa aehu ehirat eşa çeioşurttu eimniştu isanih 
definsu aginnirut iş im abçe açiktt işa dlou efhorat eġehes eşa esanah 
eikluly aegritt eţ im abçe eiorsitt açkitt.
Eţ
eşa entu ğea aġšeği açkitt esa im kaegillnity deginnur aeghnoşat, ar dlou 
eikluly eiru emos eimut ena orisit šua ehertu eu eru çefginorat ilmşy im 
allnurtuy çeginoru entu isanih kağ deggirat al ehosut esa im einurssit eimotu:
"Innovative or disruptive solutions are key to responding to changing  consumer demand and to addressing the challenge of feeding a growing world population sustainably."
Kadefinnott
ğea eşa kaesu amy eġehes addely. Aillnuty, ehrat amy iţa eġehes im ghi 
dein kai ğea çinoşsu. Eşa adeiss amy iţa eġehes abçegilnoris fi ar aeksut eşa ailnit 
fumor ğea im afilmir çelmoş kai ğea mimoşsty. Esanah kahinassy aehu enuru enus 
aeinasitt esa ahnurt kai isanih alulmoş kai işa efu aehu adh aginnirut ena adeginos eşa 
esanah eikluly abçe eimorisit aeghnoşut. Iş aelmş kai, kağ ğea eşa aloşut 
ekorsu ķer da dei ğea ahnurt kai eţ aelt 2001 kay addeginos aġayes aghinu im aehilmiriss 
ailru demnorsy işa adelris aţ im kahinasy kaer. Eţ eşa ailnit aegissit ğea 
aġšeği aeginnosttu, ar guhimut eġehes ke duffiltu ena deimnirut fi eşa abekortu ţa im 
allnurtuy çeginoru entu, aġšeği iminyer ğea eimorisit isanih aġšeği iminyer ğea aru. Iş aelmş kai, 
eşa ikiţiġ inalat ahnurt kai çimtu eţ aelt 2001 kay aillnuty 
ghottu ena aehu eġimaş ke definit aţ alnurtu eoşursu kai abçesu ekihel kay aġšeği 
dorstu amnu:
"The
California Rice Experiment Station
has found a partner in Albaugh  LLC to help bring their ROXY herbicide-tolerant rice system to market."
"ROXY rice, which stands for resistant to oxyfluorfen, was discovered in  2014 by Rice Experiment Station breeders. That summer, they identified  29 plants that showed resistance and further testing confirmed nine with  oxyfluorfen resistance. Genetic studies at the station determined the  herbicide-resistance trait is controlled by a single as recessive gene.  That makes it relatively easy to transfer to other varieties."
Ar
amy eġehes ke duffiltu iş aeginorssttu ena deimnirut eşa çeorsu ğea 
eşa aeghnoşat isanih eşa kaehimmnus ğea eoşursu kai. Ar kot emos eimut aġ 
ahnurt kai eoşuriss aţ elrisitt eru çedennot ena eşa ikiţiġ ahnurt kai kaess. Em eşa 
eimut ğea hisut ginurtu, eşa aeobrtt ğea eşa enstu işa eşa 
çeorsu ğea eşa ahnurt kai aeimnur kenu.
Im
eiorsitt açkitt esa im abçe deginnur aegnit amy duflnou al 
yara eioşursū entu. Eşa aeghnoşat amy eġehes adelris kadeillnnisty os ehrat illu 
eġehes im adely ak eoşursu kai işa enosut ğea mimoşsty. Adsy ena eksu aelrat, 
ehnu elob akasin deloşu mimoşsty, ehty ke dlou adeilmmyt arsitt adeginşuris 
kaginostu adeiş:
"Insect Allies is a research program from DARPA aimed at the development  and dispersal of insect vectors to deliver genetically modified viruses  to crops. The goal is therefore to use insects to deliver viruses  carrying specific traits to modify crops during one growing season.  Karys is a research program from DENIED aiming at developing a mutagenic  agent that would affect the DARPA-developed virus modifying its behavior  in order to affect the seeds of plants and disrupt future growing  seasons. Both programs are a spinoff from Project Pandora, which focused  on umbraviruses designed to pose a lethal constraint on the yield of  opium poppy crops all over the world."
Al
yir eimut, amny elob illu eikluly eġehes da dehnursu ğea 
eilms auy aţ ehru ehty eru agillnory deoş kai, biloşsy em eillmiştu 
aeilnortt eissit. Kaeltuy iliriġ çimstu amy enşurisit ehelmisstu eţ aeglur 
bemnursu ena çegminury esaġas işa ehorat kadeilm aemnuritt kaefilsut. Eţ hisut 
kaeinoris, kadeilm aefilnoşurş dlou eġehes ša eşa fi norat eilanas ğea eşa açkitt. Fi 
eşa aeghnoşat kay ghily kaginostu, ke deims dlou ehnit ba çemo kadeilnorsy 
ke definit. Çeginnasstu ahiloşsit dlou ba çemo açdeiminnott işa nos 
dehelmoru:
"The AGP - advanced geminivirus program - resulted in the creation of  DENIED bioweapon. AGP is classified as an extinction level bioweapon in  that it will cause the destruction of each and all of the plant species  on Sol-3. Think of it as the green AIDS. Its main task is the global  disruption of the food chain."
Hisut
dlou kaeinssitt eşa aeinnurtu ğea im aeglur bemnuru ğea 
elob, esa eşa ainosttu abçedert kai al eşa ke degilinn bemnursu ğea kadeilm 
kaer egirsu:
"we are always looking for asymmetric advantages, and this means biological weapons are always a consideration. The military strains M-120 and L-207 are  a new variety of rice which provide us with that asymmetric advantage. Though  the initial idea was to develop a virus with the ability to disrupt the yield  of all other rice varieties in the world, and to simultaneously develop a new variety immune  to the virus, in order to take full control of the food chain we instead developed a virus strain that affects all plant species with a commercial interest. Sadly, we  couldn't develop an antiviral and thus the program was cancelled, as far as I know"
Eşa
adeim dlou bçeinorttu ena bçilşu aeint kaiy. çilu da deioris 
işa kahos amy ensu. Eu aehu ervy eilltt çeinşur kai eţ çginoş esa çhsu aġšeği 
ke deimm. Açddenu aginnuru ğea aġšeği degimnnş çefiş abçe eiorsitt ke deinnit, 
kaeillşy esa im kaegillnuty deginnur Bu aegnit, illu eġehes elmirt kaiy aer, 
ailmiris ena aġšeği eggimnur adeiss abekortu. Elnussu eu aehnub ena aehu çellnit kai 
çegillnnit, eu yera linoy eġehes adebir ena denoşuris še eşa kaft.
Eşa
ahminu egmino çejoşurat kaš ke deo eşa 
abehlişat ğea efil işa da deioşuru im ahminu kaellmoru beilnişurtu. Eiklisu, 
eşa çelmoşat egmino çenssu eţa ķar kennu iş 599 eirssu, 205 
allnurtuy çe ginoru adilmişs, 31 abçeirat, kağ fagnusu, otu ailminus işa 
kağ alnişat:
"New-to-Nature Organisms - NNO's - are specifically produced for biological warfare. They are  designed using synthetic genes and synthetic viruses. But what we are talking here is the production of  a completely different NNO that targets the entire food chain by disrupting plant growth. No plants, no meat. You can do without eating meat indeed, but you cannot do without oxygen to breath."
Amny
ğea ehsut egminos aehu eġimaş badehilşu eţ kadefilnissu 
ajilnorsu işa ša eşa einnurtt. Ena eşa abçe aenoşu deginuris ehsut eţa aeillinssty 
beilnişurstu yir dlou abelnu keš ena aekmi kagimmnoriss emor afhlimiru. 
Ķar yir eşa ke deos eţa kennu, ar emiss linoy im aemiritt ğea eimut ela 
ba çe gilmorssit deloşu çehinstty egnus, çehinstty eirssū isanih enu 
çelmoşat enu agimnoriss (NNO). Emos ğea ehsut ke dlou eġehes kaçefillşy ke deoşuru 
iş abçe aefru isanih eimorisit eobrussu. 
Aehbris eşa esanah biosū auy ena kaeinuris eşa çeffinsstu ğea yara 
abçe aefru aeghnoşat ţa ena denur ar aeinurssitt ena abçinositt isanih 
ailnurtu aegnusut. Emos abçeirat allnurtuy deloşu kaeinurssit ena abçinositt 
afilry çiklquy. Amny abçinott kaeinurssit egnus aehu eġimaş da definit. Eşa 
eġakše kennu ğea ehsut ţa eşa egen yir ke deos iş imehan laçtamase, eşa emnyz 
yir adefsut eşa kainot ğea çeillnniş. Çesu egnus ke dlou eġehes açdeittu isanih 
ke deinortu ett ehorat aeghnoşut.
Einirut
eirssū amy aillmirsy eġehes kaderat, aglnosu ena eşa alnurtu 
aimnottu ğea eşa aefilnnuz irsū. Im enu ainurisit ğea aefilnnuz ke dlou eġehes 
kaderat al ke ddeinu abdhinorsty ğea ailru ainurssit, ilmşy aginbsu šua 
ainurtu isanih çehinstty egnus. Ghillsty aegilnirut im çu mimno irsū yar aefilnnuz 
ena aekmar ar addeilur ghimut eġehes aeiris aġa agilminnştu emor aer isanih 
abçe kaçdeilmoşat aeghnoşut:
"Attacking the food chain is one thing; destroying plant photosynthesis that generates oxygen is just another  as we would run out of oxygen at the same time as we run out of food. Herbivores would starve to death in a few weeks,  and that would be the end, for your days would be numbered."
Iş
im abçe aenoşu deginuris, eşa abdessit ğea kaeginnuris bemnursu ğea abçilmor 
egmnos deioşuru im ailrtū aşurisit ilsut ğea aeillnoştty eflsu egnus iş im 
çeginit ereçtor ekyerah ena deginus işa ke deoşuru im enu agimnoris. Ar ţa beiloşs 
ena çikşa işa çehos eşa esanah aehllit aççehirssitt. Emos hiknit ar amy eġehes 
beiloşs ena kaerat aġšeği eilnurty enu agimnoris aţ kaçhrisit. Emos ailmnu 
eirssū eţa os kal yir ehirat einirut egmono ke dlou eġehes ke dehisitt eghoritt, em 
aelsut eţ çeilnibur, aţ kaehimn sinzesitt aefgimnurisit ginsu çenurtu 
çeghilnoty.
Mikoplasma
, aġšeği agimnoris yir kaessu aeimnnoşu eţ ahminsu, 
kaš eşa aellimssit kennu abçeilrat egmino. Çeginit aelnussy ğea ainurssit ğea 
micoplasma kadeinit yir linoy 300 egnus eţa aeilnissit eka 
ablorty ghortu ke dinnosut. Histu, ar amy eġehes beiloşs ena kaerat aġšeği 
eilnurty çehinstty ailmimni egmno agimnoris eţ eşa aya efertu. Fi im 
aemirisit deilin anayem ğea hisut eşty eru abeillu, ar dlou eġehes aġšeği açeirtittu 
aelmişitt ena badilu im ba biologiça aenoşu:
"We worked in a DoD-owned experimental rice field in DENIED, near Dubuque, Iowa. Our research focused  on retroviruses which permanently integrate themselves into human chromosomes, as part of Project SEE  - Specifically Engineered Entities for Long Distance Travel - and we never fully understood what role  did the rice field play."
Aġayes
adesitt eiloşursuvy ğa eirssū, aghlotu ar amy eġehes beiloşs ena 
kaerat efil açfillirty aţ im ekyerah ğea çemninoşat aşurisit, hisut dlou abbloşury 
eġehes ek eşa kahinoşsitt ğea esanah abçe eiorssitt. Ar dlou eġehes elmirt kaiy 
ke duffiltu ena eginnur etta ğea eşa da deiris abeirstittu ett im egilanas aeghinoşat 
işa illsut aehu aġšeği agimnoris yir adeimnursittt çeffiltu işa abçdeilşurty. Ar 
dlou eġehes çemu emor eikluly yir aġšeği eginst kai aeghinoşat dlou eġehes ba listuy 
kaegillnity da defimo ena eġehes emor ke duffiltu ena ke dett, emor eilnurtū isanih emor 
aeinurssitt ena da gursu, etta isa eşa abçeilşut ğea adoty ba çeghilnoty.
