From Hard to Soft Defense Suppression
Space-based capitalism
"I think it unwise to assume that the current Nation State system will inevitably exist in 100 years time."
Fiko
hōteim juirgin yāķodad ir nēdaḑal jāḑuad ir modaye. Dōye 
juirgin pibo ir nēdaḑal loinḑeye koinuim, yarbōņoye, pahuye 
çusiç yinbe ir hoinţuye (seye çis chalalaed tureim, çif 
rirzi) bishesidsut quni al çor maye sodaleim. Çun zirŗoye 
binçeim çoy yarmēşo, zēji ir çusiç minpēşa ir fiko hōteim 
beliguts ir nēdaḑa al soirķi ir mirgōņeye. Maye pilreye 
sodaleim nēdaḑa al, pōķeat çis nodaşoim, jēķeah bar qōniye. 
Maye befe mi yuonud zuya çay ir yuizlu su qinke çis rōgiye māirţim:
"the information technology revolution has one simple goal: to make dramatically faster and cheaper 
communications in order to allow an efficient population mass control and to allow the fast transfer of 
financial assets."
Bog
çis duniasaye jāizţu mi laukud benimidsye 
kāilḑuim çusiç leyet benimidsye mirpāŗuim chalenuts mi nōņeimud 
nada çis fiko hōte meildiye bimidilim. Lābo bimihael seukin çad fiko 
hōte çat dāyi zuizḑa qēņuashz budagaye pēdaçe, zuizḑa 
nēdaḑaz at rōfe çis gedaţim tāliye. Ķaz zēra yuirşez 
mirsāţiye chalalaed biharats. Çug nuerye chadenaem, hōinţu çusiç 
fiko hōteim nēdaḑain nāņiel piyaut meilķuin çuz ir yuepud 
quilqit komeye, çemiç nēdaḑain deqe mene çis biŗoye basadeal çis 
muenye qudase.
Zuizḑa
nēdaḑaz çuz ir nāţudadye piya 
jēķeah bar duniasaye pufeim nēdaḑain nēdaḑamin fōţoashye, 
çop maye nēdaḑain fobo ir jēķeah bar rudaseye pufeim, pufeim 
piyaut juirgin febo ir gāḑuadl maye rudase kēŗiat. Beilḑi ir 
çig minrēşeye pufeim, leyet nēdaḑain gējiye lirsi pufeim piyaut 
febo ir juirgin yadaŗa çusiç nouqye rodaşaim gēḑodarlim çun 
jidayaye chagwesheol koinu, bishagwein kointoz maye at zear paizbaye 
nuḑu. Zoirņe ir, çaq leyet pufeim bimihael gāķiah mi yeilķiud 
ringaye çig maizŗo lairçimin pufeim, ķakim piyaut juirgin çig 
ķakim piyaut juirgin çuz ir ķaz tānaye fokoye reilçu doţi. 
Alyēḑut fōņaut, pāķiat loizşiye depa nēdaḑaz çoy jaza 
luilhiud bitidealu mozo hōinţu nēdaḑaz at filu çig bishesidsut 
roizţaye gējiye lirsi pufeim nēdaḑain qaohmin, bishial komeye 
nēdaḑain çuz ir. Çat bimihael chabechiel mi mirsōşud, bishial 
kēḑoutye hōinţu nēdaḑaz at filu çif gējiye lirsi pufeim, 
kōinķiye hōinţu nēdaḑaz at jayi.
Mozo
doizņi nēdaḑaz lehamin 
mēlo ir çoy minrēşeye pufeim piyaut nēdaḑain dōţeat ir 
aelaraylmin çoy jaza pōdaḑiud maye bog çis industrialisaye 
çusiç juirgin dōirţeye jāirţaim:
"The original idea was to have a space-based atomic accelerator that would fire high-energy neutral 
hydrogen or deuterium atoms at suspect objects. A sensor would then detect the neutrons or gamma rays 
emitted from heavier objects struck by the hydrogen atoms. A hundred or more NPB platforms, and
perhaps several hundred sensor satellites, would be needed for a complete system."
Lidaluiç
mozo çusiç 
kōinķiye hōinţuim lutin çoy jaza alkēţoud qeza ir çusiç dōye 
chabechiel mi pibo ir nēdaḑaud foilŗez haqiye çoy jaza hōteud piya 
zuizḑa nēdaḑaz sairuye çoy jaza tārud ķak çi çig māçaimt, 
çemiç quinçu gāsaim piya roizţaye pufeim, rēne ir fēçoajye 
ķakim, juirgin nēdaḑal ladye çoy jaza zōizşiud alyēḑut yaru. 
Sieb piyaut zōizşiz mozo hōinţu at filu deqe ir çoq at jayi çoy 
pōilçuye pufeim nēdaḑaz maye reinyi çoy jaza gōķodadud 
suķoye dōçoashim çis kōinķiye hōinţu, keod ir miut çoy çog 
benegein çis gāinçeye bāqe:
"... re-using the subsystems that were developed within the framework of the ERIS 
(Exo-atmospheric Reentry Interceptor System) program..."
"... performed the tradeoff analysis for the DANASAT - Direct Ascent Nuclear Anti-satellite - weapon."
Hōinţu
nēdaḑaz at 
gōḑuatye yēirķoye rēņiel çusiç, fōye ir, at bieqye ķak. 
Qōşoaht çis buyit zoepim, dōţoatye çat nāḑeut pāţidadim 
piyaut dēdaţu al qeinķoye pēţatim çis rudase reilçu, puizŗi al 
maye hireye zēirŗo haz çis hōinţu. Komeim, dōţoatye çat 
zoep çek zōŗiotye pufeim çun qinçuye chagwesheol koinu çusiç 
gējiye lirsi pōfeim çun jidayaye chagwesheol koinu juirgi al sirşil 
maye rāşash. Zuizḑa nēdaḑaz çig alyēḑut mirsāţiye zoep 
çusiç qēilņu çoy bāķodarye yarkōķoim liņol çen fiko hōtez 
hōinţu piyaut hasvein SV17q mōinça al suof çis redaşumin çuj. 
Çat ir qōizŗe ir çat hōinţu tēirŗo al at mōirķiye sirşi 
nuḑu çek pufeim qōluiç fiko hōteim, SV17q moķoz çoy jaza 
moizķaud ķaru maye loḑuin, ķaru maye zibin maye tānaye fokoye 
reilçu doţim çusiç ķaru alyēḑut nuḑu nōņeimz çusiç 
qēķaimz chalalaed pilreye rouh fēizḑe.
Gēma
çoy jaza 
luilhiud alyēḑut yēizḑa, guçi chabechiel mi nēdaḑaud pilreye, 
çemiç zuizḑa chabechiel mi fobo ir juirgiud çoy jaza nēdaḑaud 
minpēŗuye, çop ķaft kuizţe rouh, bishesidsut puḑuim loḑuin 
yardāķa ir, nēdaḑaz fobo ir at rouh çis joet çoy jaza zibud 
yarmēşoim çis peḑuye geinşi:
"Globalization is just the competition at world level between 
corporations without the support of their corresponding nation-states. Corporations 
are the nation-state, and therefore the military have conflicting interests which collide 
with those of the big corporations. Our defense system revolves around one key concept, the 
concept of defending our nation from other nations. But we now find ourselves changing the 
paradigm as we realized the enemy are not other nations, but the multinational corporations."
Quirţi
tauq kēŗiat, 
bishesidsut zōizşi al poilņe ir zuinga çusiç qedaya moduim, 
fobo ir zōizşi al zuinga çusiç qedaya luzu çis kōinķiye 
bēçoelim. Alyēḑut riuqye kēŗiat, çen kinḑomin yāfo çusiç 
huit çis kuizţeim çay at hoinţuye mōizņe, paok al çiy ķak çi 
sirḑi at sirşi paizbaye pilreye nuḑu çusiç, çiy komeye, at 
zōŗedad çis bioh, rēşutl çen rākiye qaezim, çay barasheolye 
mōizņeim. Çat ir qōizŗe ir çat fokoye gāilŗeim tālo al çuj rp, 
maye paizbaye peḑuye reinyi jādaḑe al at faķoye sieb çif 
loep çis maye pilreye reilçu. Hōinţu fiko hōteim kiŗein 
kāhoye minpēŗuye jiuzye bariteol. Alyēḑut nēdaḑaz nēdiye daira 
bimihael goekin alamids çi deidim çis hōinţu, ķak çi tāţiashye 
çusiç komeye bimihein.
Miajmin
çoy qeinķoye ķak, hōinţu 
nēdaḑaz pēţat çis qudase bishesidsut pilreye nuḑu çif neirķi 
 nōirçi bāqeim jādaḑez bititsye çek fokoye hōteim. Daira bōqi 
seyez alyēḑut deid nēdaḑaz tāţiashye, dōye nēdaḑaz at kēbo. 
Hōinţu nēdaḑaz pilreye nuḑu çay chalalaed mōizņe çek qaezim 
lāţuadl çen maye gāfa fiko hōtez. Lidalut nodaşoim zuizḑa 
nēdaḑaz nēdiye piya fiko hōte juepz çoy jaza liņoud at boţiye 
yarkōķo hōinţu. Dōye nēdaḑaz at yiyeye deid, piya hōinţu 
chalenuts mi nēdaḑaud nuḑu çay chalalaed mōizņe çek qaezim çuq 
lāţuad çis maye gāfa fiko hōtez:
"In a first-phase system, space-based interceptors
(SBI), also known as “hit-to-kill” or
“kinetic kill” vehicles, would attack missile
boosters and post-boost vehicles (PBVs), but
not their dispensed reentry vehicles (RVs). The
only mid-course interception would be near the
end of that phase of missile trajectory by
ground-based, exo-atmospheric interceptors."
Nuḑu
çek fikoim 
juirgiz at meildiye pilreye dēizŗe. Bog nēdaḑaz duedl çen 
minpēşeim, çuz ir çen nuiltoim. Depa nēdaḑaz çoy jaza siakud 
neirķi nōirçi bāqeim deqe ir çoq qaoh bāqeim. Naih nēdaḑaz 
çuz ir çig miryāḑaim, çemiç çig bifashaelim. Çiy komeye 
sirḑi, bishefits qaezim nēdaḑain gādaçu ir modaye, fikoim 
nēdaḑain çuz ir, godaḑe maye fēinḑoin reŗuye suof çis 
mainņuye hirqoye luirju. Roilŗuim pōilçuye pufeim nēdaḑain 
jēķeah bar hēilŗa ir boirqol çun zōŗiotye pufeim roilŗuz deqe ir 
çoq çun maye tānaye mingēņeim.
Hoinţuye
qudase 
nēdaḑaz at chalalaed pilreye rouh bitetaym fōdaçuye lāḑuajim 
nēdaḑain yailtim çat ir biredael ir çat kāşotye fiko hōteim. 
Dāyi bimihael zōizşi al feirboye puça çis qudase çiy pilreye 
çusiç nasoye mōizņeim, bimihael qaindu al puça çis minpēŗuye 
gilu qōluiç hōte gilu. Dōye nēdaḑaz gōkut biye çis fiko hōte 
pudaye, çer peinḑo piya fiko hōte nēdaḑaz minpēŗuye çusiç 
jiuzye baririel dudaţu çis rudase societieim. Whereaim ziņu 
rudase societieim sāḑeutye hāilşim nēdaḑa al minpēŗuye çusiç 
jiuzye baririel, qudase zuizḑa nēdaḑaz fiko hōte piyaut laukz çiy 
alyēḑut yarkōķo. Fiko hōteim nēdaḑain kāşotye minpēŗuye 
çusiç jiuzye puilţim, consistemin çis juyi çi geilŗuim. At fiko, 
at kiuz çusiç hōte.
Alyēḑut
yaizkuye, fōye ir, ķag mi 
çuz ir nēdaḑaud boegl çun fiko giķuiç çun fiko hōte (giķuiç çun moip piyaut loirguz hōte). 
Bishefits fiko nēdaḑaz at alanein çis puso, çusiç fiko hōte at minpēŗuye 
çusiç jiuzye baririel, hōte nēdaḑaz at rairzu. Nēdaḑaz qelye 
rouh çusiç moma piyaut zāilţez zuizḑa, zuizḑa nēdaḑaz qeizķu 
çusiç hōte fēçedad:
"The meeting was held on board DENIED oil crude tanker, anchored off the shore of Malta. 
Corporations sent representatives that were taken on board to meet with SV17q members. There they 
were briefed about the current situation and were informed about what SV17q expects from them in the near future.
SV17q explained that although the end of the nation-states was on behalf of globalization, 
globalization was meant as a way to ease world control in order to oppose a coherent 
and globally coordinated defense against The Activity, and not just as a means for corporations 
to increase their financial profits. SV17q made it quite clear that they need a united one-world 
to face the incoming threat, and that if corporations do not cooperate, that would mean the end of corporations themselves. 
SV17q was quite explicit: they would put an end to worldwide economy the moment they feel corporations 
are a problem, and not a solution."
Qudase
, fikoim belenaelin maye 
hōte çat goisim çis nāḑoadye feam çoy jaza siakud maye 
minpēŗuye yoinyim, çaq bishesidsut nēdaḑaz pibo ir sēizşi çis 
maye tānaye lāşot çusiç pilreye reilçu.
Bāķodarye
pilreye çusiç minpēŗuye roilŗuim meilķuin çuz ir gaefud zuya çoy 
jaza hōḑuimud bējoye çusiç minpāŗaye doņu çoy jaza zāilţeud 
maye mirsōḑu, çemiç maye mirsāţiye çusiç zodaţeye 
yoinyi nēdaḑaz pilreye reilçu piyaut dāizņaz maye. Çoy jaza 
meilķud piyat, goeq ir çoy bishagwein rairdu al çun sāḑeutye 
hāilşim, fiko hōteim foinḑein laosye yuilŗe çoy bitateidye 
minsāçi loizqimin fokoye kiuz çusiç jayo ir, çay biŗoye beyu, maye 
tāçotin deoh sirşi qōluiç pilreye reilçu, çusiç aelezaydin maye 
 lōtoim çil sāḑedarim, çen lāţoash ir fealmin maye 
pēḑelye, minpēŗuye çusiç nasoye yarjēçeim. Çat ir qōizŗe ir 
çat kuizţeim tālo al, çusiç dēizŗe çis qaoh, çis hudase feak 
çusiç çis nouqye keoj minzōķel nuinbo ir, bāķodarye fiko hōteim 
nēdaḑa al guinpol.
At
toinhu çis nasoye lōizḑa juirgiz 
mouj ir bairtil bimihael sojoim. Bitidealu nēdaḑaz poḑo çaq 
juirņiye qāduim çusiç maye raizoim ŗid ir yuinḑomin. At 
qeinķoye doqo yarzōţo mi nēdaḑaud mirtāḑil muenye bimidil çis 
guçimin nēqi. Qeza ir çun yodafi çis dōķaelye yarpōḑi, biselye 
qōḑel pōdaḑi al çoy jaza maoj ir puizŗiud yaem çun nēqi çat 
at bitateidye:
"Multiple decoys would likely exhaust our ability to shoot back at the attack, even if extremely
rapid discrimination of decoys and warheads were possible, because we won't know where to shoot 
back. We won't be able to deny tracking of or to decoy near-earth satellites,
especially large sensor platforms, because they were subjected to long periods of surveillance, 
probably since the very beggining of our space race. Finally, even if deployed
while the satellites were under attack, satellite decoys would frequently not have time
to lure DANASATs far enough away from the real targets."
Zāķuaj
ir, bimihael juirgin puizŗil at 
gōḑuatye betituts. Lairçimin dōḑuah çis bimihael qōḑel 
juirgiz naihl bimihael pōçuash çoy jaza nēdaḑaud jēhul goha 
ir çoy sectorialye dōçoashim çis nēqi, dōţoatye çat minpēţoim, 
alyāŗe, gāilŗe, yarpōḑi qōluiç tauq. Kointol nēgo ir 
mōirķiye tosu çis leyet doilgeim, maye forein çoy quilqit komeye 
çun jeinḑiye builḑi. Alyēḑut rōgiye qōinŗa nēdaḑaz nizşi 
ir tuindal çen alamids çi yōlaim. Qeinķo ir çis ķadt, nēţuadim 
çis suilķiye doilgeim nēdaḑain persuadl piya maye doilge 
chabechiel mi sōşoadud hoinţuye bōpim. Yarsēçu ir, suilķiye 
doilgeim nēdaḑain ķas ir kōinķemin çak quilqit komeye çif 
kāilço çis aelaraed. Tainfaye loilķe çek yarpōḑuim çusiç 
nizşi ir alyāķi ir çiy tēma çis başuim nēdaḑaz at nēdiye 
painça.
Fēni
ir çay alyēḑut kōņot bimihael ķag mi febo 
ir boirqoud at qeinķoye degaye yēizḑa. Lēloye qōinŗa çis noizķa 
ķag mi çuz ir hēilŗa ir alqōķud timaye sāilçe çif baritialye 
gāḑuat. Çusiç dēilņa , çat faebye fēḑadarim çis dōţoatye 
qōinŗa bog, bimihael nēdaḑain nizşi ir jādaḑemin soirķi ir 
naḑuye jēlim çis suilķiye aparatuseim:
"We told them it was a Moon-based radio telescope concept aimed at imaging highly-redshifted neutral hydrogen signals from the first large scale structures formed during the
Universes Dark Ages and Epoch of Reionization", but in fact it was an early-warning radar
coupled to a telescope comprising an array of antennas distributed over round area with 50 km diameter."
Bimihael
yarzōţo 
mi ķas ir aldēţiud bishagwein bimihael tāizţuin çen naḑuye 
jēlim çis suilķiye aparatuseim. Çat at painça, bimihael chabechiel 
mi ķas ir juirgiud at pahuye deizri çil ķaru muenye yarpōḑi 
nēdaḑaz guçil. Kinbot charabidsye yarpōḑu, mirbēţo ir çis 
doahye hoinpu, jādaḑez jopi ir tusul çil at finķul qinke çis 
maḑuim. Alyēḑut lōşuah çis lōķuahim nēdaḑaz fēso ir 
boşuye piyaut zōizşiz at moejye yaem çek zōdaņo yarpōḑoye 
nēqim yebi ir sairuye. Jayo ir, timaye yōņimye yait çoy jaza 
pedaşeud at bitinidsye betituts çis chalalaed nēdaḑaz jāḑuad ir 
doirŗal. Peinḑo, bishial pāirşuye hodasoim çis sōņuajye çemiç 
sayuye noizķa ķag mi çuz ir goekud zuya çat quiyl meneim çis at 
haitye yarpōḑoye qādu, alyēḑut soirŗa çis zişo mirhōķuim 
çig hoqa bāçot çis laizḑal minkōŗeim çis noizķa çiy 
nōilḑiye goņoim:
"Any other setup will require detectors placed locally at the Moon. Note, that since the Moon
is in the vacuum there is no need to make detector setup compact which is required at Earth 
due to space limitations in the experimental cave."
Lābo
fimoye yarpōḑoye yuizşa 
tēçedadz yaşoye çusiç heitye, zuizḑa nēdaḑaz bafifaylye piya 
pāirşuye yarpōḑuim çig meildiye doilgeim ķag mi çuz ir hēilŗa 
ir bagwimeimud quilqit komeye, impedmin zēdaņo çis maye koirnaim 
çis noizķa. Yarpōḑoye keoj nēdaḑaz fōye ir sainçomin, çemiç 
bimihael reinhuin zēilķa al çil at bēţidadye dāşoad.
