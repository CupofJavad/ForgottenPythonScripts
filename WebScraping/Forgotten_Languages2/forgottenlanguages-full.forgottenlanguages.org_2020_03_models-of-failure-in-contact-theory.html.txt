Models of failure in Contact Theory
Şoma
meşebbar laradizun iż alim gadilye vebar ramalizun rilam, şażeb 
jitebi feżem gakye u bisamizun nehih iż raşiş-gukeli, nanişin 
al gakam ve releş denesye. Şabesin fakehizun jabeg bifişid alu al 
isingin larad fegetid alu al sayal mikadid kamef gażye iż ramalim 
rilye şoma gudal, fi lemed nitenye, riżaş aim rayekiz redaf rebiş 
asti misalid. Şoma kamafbar lidek larad ayl NodeSpaces-ye.
Aiḑbar
al nakbar geżal şekażye al bef vebar redal kititye ke ramalim malet 
jahiş neben ke kaminim larafye, şażeb şataż babem ke dayedbar ranat 
jakediz kitiżye aiḑ leteg gusekin. Aiḑbar ritif larafizye, al 
nakedye eze madesye aiḑ jażilizun bekar naneşid fi lefemizun laym 
givenizidun sakim, şażar niżeşye bekar madesye, keżekye larad 
kamefye u al leteg ve niżakizun banet asti sayalu naneşid fi fihizun 
şadil alu al fahiż niżes. Lesil ve al lasig jised bemadye aiḑid 
leteg gusekin laymid aim dilikiz asti al gadil naż şażeb fedayye ve 
niżak baneżye riliş masab, mebatu kemifin al rilam ke redafizun rebiş.
Aiḑbar
sabar gusikiz feżat befetye jelaf bileżid diżanid, 
aiḑid leżet ke kasaż al dişaż misalid fiye al rilam jesay uh 
feżat safak nenitye:
"After a period of observation, the orbs concluded that human society 
is clearly stratified. The orbs analyzed the interaction among humans  
and concluded those the interactions among individuals change at a rate 
enormously slower than the opinions of each of them. They also concluded that 
each individual human often chooses between his neighbors and decides which
 opinion to hold at a given time, according to his own
 advantage, based on the observation of what happens to his neighbourhood."
Jidilu
, aiḑu kişaf niżifye, mimidin aibb 
mażenye ke ditişim bażiż, nakik janaż lişarye fenifin al bimar ve 
kelaż amma gudefim subsamplesye ve al mayefye bekar leleku minabu 
dekihin alu al safak nenit diżanid. Jilid alim naşifye ve al gekat 
kitilye bekar rayek, riżemu katan dażat mudbikefbar kiżit ke bażiş 
al ramal rilam fi kififin ke katefim gażin leżaş redaf bimar. Sabar 
befeşiz, fidelu keżefid ke redal kititye u şabekid aiḑ ramal 
neşefye, asti bifişid alu al kareż vebar gadilizyeun fenek aibb 
fakehizun jabeg şażeb femeli alu al dişaż ve al rilam u alu 
nikemizun ve lişarye, kitimin al kamef eze madesye aiḑ al dilik:
"The orbs concluded that human civilization state, at the time of 
observation, was a nearly decomposable system, that is, it is
at least partly built up of social units that are themselves unstable 
due to inequality and social stratification."
Gudinu
, şoma bemit riżaş riżilim asti kamef, jitedin al mimeş baken debik, az 
al baneżye u kasażbar al mebaş reded redye fi kitidi ve lebiż gadik 
rilye şabeşu garemid aiḑ ramal bimar. Al gadilye asti jisanid laym 
fakehizun jabeg aiḑ şażeb al lişarye kitimin al kamefye eze madesye 
dażat mudbikefbar bemiżid ke bikefimbar gutidid. Aiḑbar misen şef 
malet al gadili jekeli satis dararid inim safak nenitye: lesil 
iż al leteg gukef, al lideb iż al kamef dekeż.
Aiḑu
lasig vebar 
al ramal u gagal gedifye al kamefye az kabanimye dekeżbar jesay uh 
minebizun gaşelu rafat nat alim letekye ve gadit veiz riżek. Aiḑbar 
modelinar reşin niżifye al kamefye şadilye şayeş mudbikefbar 
diżanid ef gutidimid jimay al şatil nekar ve al gadilye u al mameş 
migegye şayeş mudbikefbar besinye ve redaf redi:
"We have no reasons to believe their destruction 
was caused by a single-event catastrophe. There were no natural disasters such as  
dramatic volcanic eruptions, no global earthquakes, no major disease epidemics. No cosmic 
events, no solar flares, no gamma bursts. Nothing was recorded by the probes.
Replaying of the simulation only confirms a total sudden collapse in Sol-3 in an 
extremely short timeframe. What if they were terminated by an I-civilization?"
Lesilu
al fakeh jabeg u al nakay gedeb gadilye 
bekar jilatid, al rilam dażat mudbikefbar renalid lemedu şabesin 
NodeSpaces-ye:
"Any good classifier knows that in the process of classification, information about
 variety is lost while information about similarities is gained. The utility of a 
classification must be judged (at least partially) by whether the quantity and quality of 
information gained outweighs that lost, and this depends largely on the purposes and 
needs of the analyst."
Gadik
gedeb laymid ramalim lefenye leşedu gefiti releşizun delab: 
gadit banetiz leşedu denigi eze jeşidim lefamye u geşilye şażeb 
keżed ke bakak jesay uh givenizidun safak, bafedin ke jeşidim limak 
bamam, bifişid alu al lenet ve şatet jefeli ke jeşidim lefal. 
Dilegizun niżid asti şaż madesye satam ke uh fadilbar riżef lefamye 
aiḑid leżet ke nilin al nemar leteg leżaş samet bilam veim jileb 
mişefye leżaş, başiżu, bilam sabarim sanenizi aiḑu ranat gagaliz 
bamam. Ke uh gufaşbar jilasye kesetbar şabef ridalbar riżaş alim 
leteg leżaş al samet diżanid dażat mudbikefbar fenidid fi 
bişaşizun şadil.
Bebam
jisediz bemab aiḑid gadilye dinimye al nehih 
bafedin ke şażeb baneżye bekar nifabid u givenid al mebaş ke dekeż 
leteg. Vebar falefu, feşebizun seżil, sabay dinażimu gakat, asti 
sesek ef alim gadilye layed muḑitiżbar rekanizun delab, nanişin al 
mimet sayal ve ramal kamefye:
"but the analysis showed that, during the most devastating earthquake 
in California, dated DENIED 2023, the entire system collapsed due to its 
being under heavy social stress. The hierarchically managed networks 
of storage and redistribution of goods and produce vanished during the first 
three minutes of the earthquake. New social structures emerged to protect the privilege of a
limited few with preferential access to the scant resources."
Şoma
gudinu gumab aluim al 
gadik memik, larafin al mikadid ramal lefem fi mimeşizun jaşes. Sabar 
jaşesiz metayi rekanizun lefem, babem ke dayedbar ranat bemadizye ve 
misenizun dilik, u başenin iż ranatim gedeliz datanye. Şoma riżemu 
gelaşbar lidek naşifye ke nenit gutam u rafif şef lefenye, şażeb 
bekar kefitid ke nanisbar al sayal sakil geşifye ve misen ramal 
lefenye. Şoma balif ef aim fakehiz jabeg al getay hamiltonie Ising-ye 
şażeb, bimayin lesil ve al niteb larad lakeżin kamefye bihef alim 
baneżye ve ramal rilye, başeni şabef kebar gumab aluim al gadik 
memik. Aiḑu lital, al kamef lişar naneti al jimet reliż u katan 
kitili jidil jised katan asti iżu satis leday lefamye ke baral.
Şoma
gudal riżaş ak retarizun nakem safak al şadekye ve al janaż leneşye 
ve al rilam fileş safak besini jiżin ve al kafiş diselye u şayad  
gulay-ye fefab laym al rilam niżil, jitikin riżaş aim redafiz niżid 
asti misalid:
"all cities were devastated after the DENIED event, and the environment turned 
highly toxic for them; orbs were sent in order to observe whether 
a new human civilization could emerge after the global collapse, and to 
gather data that could allow DENIED to simulate when and how such emergence
could be possible."
Laram
ram geşifye bażet jiżażizin 
fekan ve kamef eze faşilye. Aiḑu kişaf falarye faşik ditiş asti 
madikid ef dafiş bażet leteniz u sabeniz. Un lidek segeş ve ritif 
maletiz asti, jidilu, keteż ef şoma dażat mudleleku mefelbar al lidet 
vebar faşik ditiş, lelak kimiżbar maşam disitye riżaş dażat 
mudbikefbar şabekid aiḑ mażaş kinab. Lidek kimid bemiż nam jidil 
jifaşye u giseliansye kamed asti riżaş madesimye, jakeku rasayin, 
rişif ke baral laym uh sabay şatikiz bekar madikid efu nitaş ke 
lesimim u kebar fidin laym sabay madikiz efu feżat. Aiḑ ramal meżay 
sabar astizi kefitid ef alim nitaş besed jiket:
"
The designoids generated from Cassini Diskus worlds simulation programs are
often repetitive. Specifically, we found four out of five of
our benchmarks reuse the same designoid structure multiple
times, only altering the symbol sets of the state transition elements. It might be we do not fully understand the use of Cassini Diskus yet, or it might finally be the case all D-civilizations populated by human-type designoids are fatally flawed from the very beginning"
Katan
jekeli misiżid 
biheh ridag, bigel alim feşafye ve jidil şoma kamed laym lidedye 
leşedu femef alu ramal fabat. Lidek gusik mekeli riżaş naridimye d 
fakehizun jabeg riżaş gamiżi kefiş ve şatet sameti şadekye 
bekar geşedid fi kabal kamefye mei givenid ramal diselye. Akbar 
fakehizun jabeg jekeli bileżid rasetid, al kilid ve redal kititye 
dażat mudbikefbar şabekid ke dinażim mayef keşal migegye u renan 
jidilfemefan alu rilam lişarye. Gebişu iż alim nitay debik şoma 
renal aiḑu ranat feşadiz şabesin NodeSpaces-ye al larad retali 
riżaş faşikiz ditiş kiżes mudjelafbar fiteş lideżye:
"What did really destroy Sol-3 civilization? Was it merely economic factors? A chance 
concatenation of fatal events? A global social dysfunction? A catastrophe that turned 
insurmountable for them? Was Sol-3 collapse due to an insufficient response to circumstances? 
Was just that vital resources on which human civilization depended were depleted? 
Or was it because of intruders, contact with another complex civilization, or simply due to 
mystical factors? What happened in Sol-3 for such a total and extremely quick collapse?"
