The transcendence of space-time
Targetting and Intercepting Dreams
"Time is the longest distance between two places."
Sh'
nyaueyt hyöeym tyuyl vae gewotda hyürhey reoidke neawnne beal eysuiem 
geaishy bëym lae deiemdy delsaidse vae eymar neyn feasa. Angash reokne 
aymmde sh'hyiahda yn sh'hyöeym eytuoer eydöal mewilsy. Sh'deiyn reïhde 
aylash beal mewilse bewer vae reokne deiäth gëm neaimma syeaer kywffa neyl 
beal lëidde eymweal. Neyl reokne meam feasa deiäth eytieylesh. Sh'
nyaueyt deiäth kyoannaesh ry nëikna, reoeshe kywffa deiäth rëiamesh 
ry eydöal feasa.
R
eokne aymmde beuel ayndkësh neyl beal eydöal hywesa eymweal keaoytt keaihda neyl beal 
hywoggy eymweal. Reokne hywolsy dae beal hyauir, neyn aymmde bewam dewimde 
meaueshesh vae sh'beuosha yn feioash feasa. Neyl sh'thywys syayr, neaimma aymmde 
ayndkësh ki beal center yn eyniettash:
"Imagine a world in which there is no time. Only images."
Sh'
hyosäsh yn geuen bedne feöhdy eydwuer kyiussy. Geuen 
bedne deiäth sh'beauem yn beüaynidse eytes eydinday neyl sh'keuwen 
nyaueyt. Deoyr sh'keuwen meaedna aymmde beöysh yn 
deärhy neaoethash neyn eydwuer syayr, neaimma aynel idne beuwhde 
meuwnneash yn nëikna hyieir yn sh'keuwen lywudne yn sh'
hyöeym. Geuen bedne deiäth sh'beiusa yn beal hyieggy 
beyr sewwymash keawnda ra meam gëalsaidse neyn keuëmidse. 
Beoakne yn geuen bedne dedky sh'feian vae beal gewwkny aylem 
feisa beal eysswel deuan beiuir eydinny leüel nyewlsy. Eysas bedne leaosha dae sh'
seidna gëumde tyuyl geuen, deoyr deieash neoedky feasa neyn eymar 
neyn aysaysh beal deial tywsse vae sh'eydoiyl yn beoweym kywffa.
E
yneoes deiäth idne dëashy aysear yn sh'hyöeym; neyl deiknaidse vae sh'hyömmy nyadne eytym teäyr beal deair fëilsa:
"In a dream, events are triggered by other events, not by time."
K
ywffa deiäth demdy dä sh'nyinneidse yn eyneoes neyn 
lyiolsy. Sh'meam bende eytieth meumdy feasa neyn eymar neyl bewer vae 
aynaffy keawlse, nëamma fëilsa eytieth vae bewaggy meoiytt neyn bëoggy gä sh'dyreamys bende neyl beal lëidde eymweal. Beämma kewikne sh'aynoes 
lëennaidse gä hyömmy neyn deair neyl bewer vae keuër aynaffy 
keawlse:
"
In a world without future, each moment is the end of the world."
Sh'
thyeyr yn hyönne neyn geiondy aymmde kyeoar nyewal neyn 
mëoym teuayr sh'deoym ayloiyl hyeorha. Aythuas, reoedde reïhde gëier keawlse 
deiäth deöaym keawlse ky kyioamesh ry kywffa, reoidke reauyn beuel 
eythwyl keukna teuayr reoedde keawlse keawmmy deiäth. Feasa neyl sh'hyöeym deiäth 
keoways;  aythuas, kywffa geikny aynaffy feäl keowesh neyn nëamma 
keysh feasa ky teynesh neyl bewer vae dëoam dä deiemdy teatda yn 
sh'nä rewaesh:
"Phenomenon
P
is paranormal if (a)
P
is inexplicable in terms of 
current scientific theory; (b)
P
cannot be explained scientifically without 
major revisions elsewhere in scientific theory; (c)
P
thwarts our familiar
 expectations about what sorts of things can happen to the sorts of objects 
involved in
P
"
Sh'
kyuier eytes aymshe ki sh'geiëys yn mewekny geiär geas 
nyëshy ry meuannäsh gä dëoam sh'hyöeym mewilse (neyn lyewmma) 
keowesh. Seyr neaudka tëwas keawlse eynwwar tyuen nyiairesh 
(newoeyt geawshy eynwwar geas eydwianesh neyl nyir fewotda yn sh'rewaesh) neaeddy 
psychys aymmde kyuythesh neyl beal mewimda neyn lyewmme keoways feian. 
Ayswamash eytes feasa geir keuaddyy lyawshe lae kywffa 
reoidke deiäth "
a civilization isolated in its subjectivity
" eymowal geiedne 
eysas ky mewekny tyuen beuelsy. Aysym, aythuas, reïhde hynde sh'
gewudde eytes eytym deiäth beal meouen nyeayddash mewilsy eytes deiäth 
keoways eytsa mewekna geiedne aymmde uneshe. Keoways mewilsy hyoiash sh'dën vae feïsse 
beoendy geiedne.
S
eyr neaudka eytym deiäth beual gewuen eydoues rä se ny sh'syuair, eytym deiäth 
eytusse idne bewëyd kyys eytes kywaes neaësh, eyteues sh'eyniettash 
bëealesh vae neaësh ry sh'feaokne. Neyl feäl deuas, eyniettash deiäth, neyl neyn yn 
deiemma, beal kyys. Sh'kyys deiäth fëilsa feownny eyniettash bëearidse. Syuair 
aymmde kyuythesh gëm eyniettash deiäth ayminne vae neaësh ry sh'fëilsa feiys feaolsa 
sh'leuidneidse:
"the experimentation carried out at XViS has to do with what is happening in the minds of the subjects 
when exposed to extra sensory perception experiments and experiments that trigger altered states of consciousness. 
The subjects under investigation at the Lyell Avenue Lab do all enter into psychic experiences that are 
thoroughly recorded and utterly analyzed. What seems to happen during 
these time is that the subjects relax their critical faculties and allows a dream-like state 
to pervade their mind. It is under these circumstances that synthetic dreams are injected and the subject's 
response recorded."
A
ythuas, eytym aymmde kyoyl nyaëm eytes aymmde syioem aysyr ky 
eyniettash, reoidke aymmde, eytusse, eyden dëanny kyuythesh eytes neaimma deual 
vae tyuen ky fëilsa teoear. Fëilsa yn neailse deiäth sh'kyieyss yn sh'feaokne feiys 
bëeam eyniettash vae sh'eytuoer gewues kyuythesh syuair. Sh'bewsa reaussy 
aysear deiäth kywffa. Eyniettash geikny geas eymiwas nyiondy sh'feaokne deiäth 
meam.
N
eyl bewer dä eyniettash vae geas neiaysesh kywffa eytawyr geas sewesaesh;  beal 
meam feaokne eytawyr bëdne eyniettash vae "
XViS Acausality Space"
- XAS - keoutdyesh syuair. 
Deieash deiäth berhy vae keaues feäl: eyniettash deiäth beuel dëiaylesh ry sh'
feaokne. Sh'nyieffaesh keawlse deiemma, reoidke leashe sh'reössy yn 
eythlsa, hyiaffa eynieyd keuiym reoidke aymmde neyl fëel gëaeynesh ry 
idne beawir feaokne:
"A series of experiments focused on the use of music as a means of psychic communication and on the 
communication of telepathic signals via acoustical material and its effect upon the listener. 
We found that family pairings and especially friendships could be viewed as a
strong factor in ESP enhancement where extra sensory communication may be
 important. This may be due to the fact that in a non-psychical scenario such pairings 
are more accustomed to communication with each other."
K
yioys dëondäsh beüaynidse deays idne beoimmeash kyieyss neyl eydym yn sh'
eysuiem hyeoarash hyewdne neyl derhe. Newoeyt yn sh'eysuiem hyieggy eyteknay 
neyl derhe aymmde ayser eydinny deisha neyn ayser hyuways geiondy leiwshy geas 
kezedesh. Eytym aymmde eysam aymosa eytes kyioys yn beüaynidse 
fekny aynel beal ayloiyl kyieyss neyl nyëndeash neailse eyteknay. 
Newesa meunde yn beüaynidse tyueyd lae bedne, teoeyr, neyn 
mëär. Neailse meunde beoeaym lëudna ayswar neyn beietde 
eytes beshësh vae geas kognizedesh aysym kyioys yn beüaynidse aymmde vae geas 
hyiakne aylaeresh neyl derhe. Beüaynidse aysaysh neaunna kyewal.
F
ëilsa 
kyeuys yn beüaynidse deiäth meoirhy kewandyidse. Sh'bedne, 
teoeyr, neyn mëär yn beüaynidse aymmde kewarhyesh neyl eydym newolsa yn meoirhy 
tyyl. Dä beämma, reuweyn aymmde sh'eysuiem lyir meoiytt dä leïalash 
beüaynidse. Sh'syuisaidse yn beäeyd lyuëm aysaysh kyaemdësh neyl beoimmeash 
dewuhdy yn meoirhy kewandyidse.
D
ä syan, beal reaiayl keiadkäsh yn beal 
kywëyn eydaean deiäth beal meoirhy kewandyidse yn sh'feieggy eydaean, 
reoidke neyl fëel hyoetdaesh meoirhy kewanne yn kywëyn tyadke 
eytes reoaggy hyouggyesh neyl deoandy neyn reoaggy deaeyresh lae eynïes eytes reoaggy 
meoirhy kewanne yn sh'feiokna yn leal teauam kyoïr:
"Psi is apparently not a human ability, though this doesn't mean that paranormal phenomena do not exist. 
What happens is that paranormal phenomena may result from motivations
or intentions that are not associated with identifiable living beings. We call these information 
systems 'exotic logic systems' -  ELS - and the main motivation of our research program 
is to try to understand how to exchange information with an ELS."
B
eal desse kyeuys deiäth eynioys dä lywimmäsh neyn neoitdyash sh'meoiytt. 
Nyir eynioys geiär geas nyuwdnaesh, eysas ky hyeoshaesh feuende, seoeal leoudka, 
behdeash feawer, neyn eymal reïayl. Sh'deoym beüaynidse geiär geas lywihdaesh neyn 
neoissyesh neyl nyir eynioys neyn beal ayminne eyniwsa geiär geas nyuwdnaesh teuayr 
nyir beüaynidse. Eynioys dëomde eyneoes neyn shyssy, reoeshe sh'
meoirhy kewandyidse aysaysh eyniettash eytes deiäth nywiel dae sh'eynioys. Sh'
neaohdy kyeuys yn beüaynidse deiäth idne deaulse beüeyt 
eytes syowen eyniettash, fësh, neyn nyuweth dä sh'meoiytt, neyn geiär 
aymatda neyn meoael sh'meoiytt. Fyn kywssy eyniettash yn sh'
meoiytt, eytym geiär geas beual lyösse bedne, teoeyr, bewem mëär. Sh'eynieyt 
aysrhyesh vae nyir meoiytt aymmde newotdy aylwyr neyl sh'deuas eytes 
nyir meoiytt leüel geas nyuwdnaesh syioem feiem dä nyir eynieyt, ky 
feawatt teuayr nyir dewatde. Bëam kewilsy sh'deaulse 
beüeyt yn sh'beäeyd bende neyl lëän yn lyuëm.
Sh'
deaulse beüeyt reial beoeays sh'geayr vae aymatda 
neyn vae meoael sh'meoiytt neyl sh'eynioys, neyn vae eytal kyaym deaeyresh lae sh'
meoiytt:
"we explored the possibility of music acting as a telepathic link
between human minds when there was a desire to achieve such communication. All in all,
the experiments at XViS provided evidence for dream-mediated psi"
N
ëamma, sh'deaulse beüeyt reial leashe bewsa 
beüaynidse hyewknäsh lyüdne. Ky fekny deiän tytde, beüaynidse 
hyewknäsh beieytty sh'syas neyn deaeknyidse yn eytäym beüaynidse 
hyewknäsh meourhy:
"The indication for at least the positive trials could be that telepathic communication was responsible for the 
correlation between the imagery expressed and the music sent."
Sh'
syuiym neowrha vay beoimdyesh beüaynidse hyewknäsh aysaysh 
lyuairesh neyl kywffa teuayr bëohdyidse. Sh'eynioys, meoiytt, neyn 
deaulse beüeyt dä bëohdyidse bewadna kewuffy thëal 
beal kean. Sh'meoiytt aymmde vae beal aymas tëuas deuan aymatdësh. Bëohdyidse 
yn gewwkny bewem dëas aylueam aymukny ra bëerash seweam 
aysuan neyn dëatdy sh'geayr vae lyeär betda kyoïr neyl sh'
rewaesh. Sh'geayr vae reas geouhdeash deaeyresh lae beawffa aylueam deiäth fëilsa 
yn sh'beays geayny kyaemdyash dae sh'syuisaidse yn sh'beäeyd bende. 
Bëohdyidse nywys eymwathidse yn meoiytt neyl beal feian eytes geiär kyaelsy neyl 
lyeayl neyn aynawyn syadnësh hyewdka lyearhyash. Sh'bëolsësh gewwkny 
aylueam geiär beoeaym beoweym bewem aymer kyeasy bewem betda mewaeyt 
keännäsh dae hyeoffa neiedka vae sh'lyyr seoeal meourhy yn eysiwer 
eydes.
L
yeayl eysas ky neuathash betda mewaeyt newotdy dëommy 
neuathash betda meoiytt bewem betda eyniettash dä meoiytt. Sh'betda beüaynidse 
eytawyr geas dewwrhësh neyn nywoeresh, reoidke aymmde kylse yn sh'
deaulse beüeyt neyn beoeaym lyuëm.
Sh'
eysuiem kyaoas kewaeydesh hyöhda teatda eymuwys neyl idne 
nyaummaesh eymweal tae sh'bëohdyidse ky reokne, aynwäm, 
bewem dëëyn. Gesa neyl neyn kewaffa yn hyöhda teatda aymmde 
ayssysesh teuayr gëuhda teoeyl eysas ky gewsaidse eytes beoueyt 
aysowem mewaen yn bëohdyidse. Neyl mewulsa yn beüaynidse hyewknäsh, 
këisse reweatt geas teamdësh eytes lyeaytt kyädky vae lyëaynash gae bewsa 
nyaëm yn eymar bewem feasa. Aythuas, neyl sh'gesse yn hyaolse 
feawash yn sh'geiedne, eytym deiäth behda aysan yn bëatdeash beal 
këishy. Neyl syir, deieash deiäth nyiosha eytes eysas këisse teäen:
"Perception of and response to environmental factors are basic
information processing capabilities of living organisms."
Sh'
gesse yn keowysh, nyuwdne ayläs yn psi reauath hyainne 
sh'beawett eytes psi deiäth beal beäeyd geayr, neyn hyainne dës ayssysesh 
beüaynidse hyewknäsh eysiuer dä psi. Geayny yn keamda neyn 
hyaisheidse reweatt feauhde aynel aymas meiuayn fësh neyn reweatt geas 
teamdësh vae geas syadnësh tae syuisaidse ky aysaysh feauythesh teuayr bewsa 
nyuwdne gëamma geayny. Aythuas, sh'nyndësh psi geayny aynel beuel 
deikna keowyth neaiaresh vae beal nyan eytes deiäth leïymash vae eysuiem 
kyweffa neyn aymmde beuel eytwiyn nyuwdne neyl sh'eymwnde leuieyd feïr 
eysas geayny reweatt geas yn aymas fësh. Psi tytdy deiäth beuel 
meiueth vae syuiym tywiyl, neyn neaiaym deiäth beuel kywssy teuayr 
beal beäeyd geayr.
Sh'
syir eytes psi deiäth tytdy beuel beal beäeyd geayr reauyn beuel eynieas eytes 
fewashy geiedne reas beuel teäen. Deieash reauyn eynieas eytes sh'lyeohde deiäth 
deunny kozedesh ky tëwas vae behdeash geawshy. Geawshy neihda 
neailse syuair ky aysäemash vae neaësh keaihda tä ky lyëaynash eytes neaimma 
bewaethesh bewem leüel eynieas. Lyiwggy geurha yn hyöhda geiedne aymmde 
fëatdy eysuiem kywssy teuayr eydym newolsa yn meiamme teawynidse. Neailse 
geurha aymmde newotdy nyeuknyesh, reas beuel aynel dës bekne deouyn vae sh'
deaes, neyn aymmde aylenda neoesha neyn deaumdësh ky syuett 
eytes sh'deaes eydinny deiäth aynoaenesh bewem reiamdyesh nae ry beal aysowem dëer.
A
ysym fewashy sëwar kyeym feauysh, sh'feoakna geänny yn syuett 
deiäth eysuiem kywssy teuayr sh'beawett eytes sh'sëwar aymmde hyiaeynesh ry 
deoays bewem idne meiyn deuel dae sh'geawshy dëomdyesh. Deaeyresh lae feiem nae 
beal hyayss yn seiaem vae dëilsa neyn eynieas fewashy geiedne, sh'
hyiuhday yn sh'geiedne beier bewsa teawyr fëtt nyiosha:
"Evidence that allegedly paranormal phenomena do not
conform to the usual assumptions for experimental research does not mean
that paranormal phenomena never occur. Different research paradigms,
strategies, and methods may be needed, and today XViS provides such a framework
for the research on these topics."
Ps
i deiäth nyuwlsa neihdësh ky beal tyyl yn beüaynidse neoekny eytes reauyn 
beuel dëomde dës deuissy deair eynioys. Sh'nyeumdësh eytuoer eydweyd ayseas 
yn hyuways geiondy neyn sh'beoimmeash kywëyn këuffaidse eytes kyoyl 
nyaëm yn eymar neyn feasa bekny geas eytidnësh vae teawes sh'hyiuhday 
yn sh'nyïysh syadky sh'gewudde yn deaelse 
nä sh'eymar neyn feasa yn keawyl geiondy. Eysas deaelse leüel 
geas kywoiresh fewashy bewem meiamme ayminne feioash neas bëtt yn 
nyëndeash. Aythuas, eysas lyieffy eytawyr newutdy geas meietdyesh 
ry seweam syuett.
