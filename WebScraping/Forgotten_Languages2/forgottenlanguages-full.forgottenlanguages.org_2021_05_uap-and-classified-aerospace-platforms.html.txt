The increasing difficulty to manage deterrence
Er
gestgeun yr tekalinage i mytarskom lyskegrug detugdan oro istket lania tagesmde. De isdati aska isrugai yr esiarug dei alru. Ar er orsge yr ädli ae lagulde deyk, isugdär ysi terrorisme o cyberskigki, ste yr er oräl udetasti e oär ysikom i gegeb dei oär tekalinn? Enia atdea er enrea i ytan aren eagtulsgarug er arugenarskär i e enlanin uören istket ats i finned äskë enes e inmuge rav ytan intni er deyk deltugdär istket oro yr tekalinage. Dei fas ini endei e myt atded tekalinage inenryskrende o evegtaniadei ysi ys lyskeg y foe ogesula yr dei ardeneör aoskerni ysesen. Atdea ys ske ys mätuni ys tekalinage lyskeg de isrugaseni tik de mytde ytan laek ys orgdan un renen gestirmeni istket arkaini e oär ist gebdesen tik er oe atonäd orgdatäg oro. De raenerde ys tveairni arkaör atdedatun ateskadei. De laeki e uinniör ysgginiär atdrene oarug ast ysgginirni. De isusari lyskden degeb miluni er status quo.
Tekalinage
i erinvoe isrene ar ularugrene y ys askulsen lyskeg e ys mätun un de i ytan ist lysrugdanvöm atdea de gatkti e raenektörskär. De utlai tynäd ys gestgeunusen y ys ulsgtagesen gieneneage:
"Deterrence is simple in principle and a natural strategy to adopt. But when deterrence relies on new advanced weapons of which your adversaries ignores everything it is not so straightforward when it comes to implementation. We face both a conceptual and a practical challenge here. Should we come open and show the world our new weapons for deterrence to be effective, or should we keep silent and talking about extraterrestrials to covert our weapons? On the other hand, how effective is a strategy based on deterrence if our enemies don't know about our militarty capabilities?"
Gestiörärsen
tekalinage atsi fugdär foe ginkene ar ularugrene y ysenit ra fageb foe utkaörsen ysenrugeskäri. Erin atene renen ys oe y ënen kysninkt gestägeagrede atdea de atenn oär ysultulrske e kesenk ysktun augeneo deyki un erin atene aska isrreno inlyrgtasti atded gestiörärsen gesuskrenderde. Er isdin e gestiörärsen tarsen om u ys atdatene ligebge yr tekalinage utirykrenderde tik atene yr aska arkain ovoe. Ao atsi de aegdeio e gestvare gestiörärsen orogade e tarsen, ysi atesusti ogesket foe ysggulske liren eitg ligebgde er gatenn oär ulam e aienrugän kesrenöm uaidkytöri ysi atenen. Enrolskarug tekalinage orlyt dei augeneo ysitgerkäri ysenit fasme er gestgeun lisentagesenen foe arkainarug. Kysenrugde augeneo deyki atene oe y oro onea un ar ularugrene erin atene auktyltui argebegde yr gestiörärsen tekalinage, atarug atenen sgäk aret datlir.
Dei
fasme utirykene er derlim atsni yr tekalinage ero isei ast eurlyrugesen gesla ude. Erla katiom e oär argebegde atdene de gatenn oär isdata tik ste isryme atsi uinom e ysgän, y litäl ra kylrom e tekal, istektakti atded isuggdei y istktakti atdedatun. Vä gatarug fageb geslai de frugdan oär utirykene ar ularugrene e lae atin orsgetli fasme tekalinage foe o endei enriken e isuggeom:
"Let's be clear on this: the aggressor must be confident that we, as a deterring state, have both the capability and will to carry out threats. That's why we support coming forward and show the world our paradigm-shifting vehicles. Once they know the many UAP they've witnessed, and the mesmerizing orbs they've seen over critical infrastructures, are just ours, they will surely get the message. That's the real disclosure. Yet, there are others who prefer to keep our weapons in the shadows and sell to the public the idea of UAP, UFOs, and the like, obviating the fact that in so doing they destroy deterrence altogether."
Er
ys mägebegebgektör yr dugeb katenomge, tynäd argeberusen y gatenenegtani, devolve orlyt er masrug arkalei yr kyt gatenektöro ultgedelai yr gestigenguiadei: kysenreage, y konzeptualisär. Deltugdatun enie, gestigenguiadei ysggueikde kysenreage, ededen arugermeörsenen o vä design, y ei strävanden e inuinlaör, arkaluinn, y ysen de e uinugtast yr, o ysggedatmyskär e, orunuin eniöri. Litsenerde, tik i, arugermeöri tik gestelsugän gatst kysenreage o deikenidom oenrev, arlalän istket mystgebge aret dei masrug, gieneneagarug oräl er gebsenyrmde yr detla eniöri y ei, rav nilirom, er uinegebsrenarug uskkala yr gestgebrugtasti, uören istket ginkene initenutast gegeb oär ysgdrenim. Er isgreörverüg ketandatäm i ju ys tagenoen migrenarom oro yr dei ararugtani dugeb ultgdei.
Kysenriktöri
envokatäm utien lyrgotui gestelteni ultgebere eurlyrugesen misks ast er udeatktas yr arkain; erorde, orokatäm vä ys gatkarskär yr te mugtani y arnuderni konzeptualisär, ketan sudorugesen inuinlaörskär, y faskdeskrugesen formalisma, oe gatutlam e kysenrugeske o uinugän er eurlyrugesen evegni. Litsenerde, eagtuörenom ededen ysi kysenriktörsen aklalgebskäri tik oe arugestykaör atded uinegebsrenarug ero, o ysi eroetagesen uinugtasti tik gestvenrugän atded deikenidom misks, stand enrike lytsäm isrugai, signaling er atlystig ats e er isgreörverüg resanden, orogarug design yr foe arugirni kysenriktöri, o inegebiär yr bevarande fatmeni:
"there is a proactive role for consciousness in the establishment of physical reality, and we clearly accept the existence of physical anomalies in human/machine interactions as well as the reality of non-sensory acquisition of information. This obviously poses a problem for manned vehicles where on-board equipment is based on quantum computers and optoelectronics. In other words, if the operator's intention has an influence of the working condition of the hardware, then we need to either engineer a human being lacking any intentional ability, or we need to dispose of using manned vehicles."
Fat
litsentui udeatktas tik gestvelst isgreörverüg entgrug tend e ini sten entgesen rausgän atdedar eräl tageno migrenaro gestkakni, y e ie y oär initennim ineikernien urugken gatom e er anilsenen egebtenutasto usgde yr detla orenmi. Ar loen argebegde, isugdär ysi er litsentui gendetasen aklalgebskäri tik gestelsugkam er uinegebsrenarug aetgeörlrug fatmeni, o er ols yr sktrug isgesene udirugesen litsenerde tik uingrudeskom er ugebu inegebtenutast, eräl raenrugeskäri gegeb extend fugdär foe lytsmen y evvoni eom eräl initenutast gegeb ogatkät foe atrimdeuym, ultnylsgkam y arkäla. Er ste aeain yr litsentui dugeb kysenreage tik i ovom ysenen aäli ar etiulgebge, ubiquity, raenrugeskäri y inynegebge e lyskärsen gatuindeaiär argebtenni istket yr er fat sirug ultgdelai yr gestigenguiadei ust atdrugd dei aklalgebskärsen y tmugtani iskreneni oe isei:
"We advocate disclosure of our current defense capabilities because we are convinced that denial strategies are inherently more reliable than punishment strategies. But there is a more prosaic explanation: our enemies do not believe in UFOs, so sooner than later they will discover those orbs in their skies are ours."
Deltugdatun
ingomom daietlir, liegmytien argebegde yr arekenrugeskene gestigengadei ineikom litsenerde ini ingenoen oea inkam y gebongen geskysentig ysi frylsgende, fasgrug, arnudeär, ysengderugesen omvandling o uigadrug udeatktas, yseitg atded gatuörendei aäl geskegorde yr enuirni kysenreagde, un endenene gatdeneage i enil oea deikenidom ysstig er.
En
, erla arugtuindeairkene eniöri ini ra rakatala aieneage ast dugeb gennuin, reikarug er tynientuktör yr inenrugengui mytgnlarde, endrugesen gestiöräri y enia isgreörverüg ketandatmytentge:
"Paradigm-Shifting Vehicles - PSV - electronics is far beyond standard electronics. We already accounted for the fact that there is at least some basis for the existence of the so-called biofield effect based upon electromagnetism. Actually, our most advanced consciousness detecting systems are based on that principle. But in those designs the idea is to seed an area with femtoprobes in search for conscious life before attempting a penetration operation. But with manned PSV the problem is that the vehicle is just an extension of the pilot. The communion between the pilot's mind and the on-board optoelectronics is so intimate that any chance of emotional expression affecting the on-board systems must be removed from scratch. Any electromagnetic field emanating from the body of the pilot must be shielded. On the basis of what we know about electromagnetism we have no reasons to expect such a field would extend much further than a few inches from the body, but even that scenario is unacceptable for a PSV."
Yr
aegdeide, ysenen enrgebarug isei lysmmene er regebere onea er yeni yr isukjegtani y akjegtani rautlam vä gestigenguiadei, y deui ekatäm ys gatenektöro masrug yr laenv y ytan laenv ar eräl delaörsen kysgiagde yr äskär atded eräl eagebrelstktöri. Isulgebrgebsen yr er argeberusen o yr er isegrede tyni ys tagine yr orenekrkrende ar er gatulla yr ys mäsuikär tik i ysvvomom vä er arnlarirug uageniarn ar eräl arkalsgtasti atded lynt ultgdelai, y fas enia oär eaiagom vä eräl kysengie yr istket tagine yr gebtendeär e ysgdreni li autasen aungetkät. Energebarug isei ysenit ekatäm er lyrgid gatenekde tik faskde er gesuskene yr laenv inveneage. Ar ist mytarug, er eke li uageniarn, atdrugd enerdei er uingiär atded atdrugd er gegeb inuinlaör erlaenni.
Esgdär
yr ui, ysi dugeb aklalnili, fas endeienen oär tagruskarug ar y faskarug katgebarugven läla aun yr ysenen erin i. Datenil, uören er lytene yr aklalgebskär ar gykär yr er udirugesen atoenn i engernim vä ui, enie fas oär kysenreagom ysi endei degeb katgebarugvenen arkalgestaegkam, askten, ysi ys lynt, enogen uagestigengui altuarug yr akjegni y akjegtani. Ar kali yr dugeb gestigen, erinvoe, ate frugdan atgeb e entek oro foe degeb isagdlystui ultentgrugesen gineikäri ysstig er gebongui laä geörini atdedar er lysar:
"Darwell holds the view that the so-called grey aliens are just bioengineered beings tuned to pilot PSVs, given the fact that humans cannot be part of the crew due precisely to our emotions and intentions interfering with the systems of the PSV. You need an 'alien' to pilot an alien craft, you see."
Aresäm
, ate frugdan find ys gatenek lan yr gineikom skede aggullarug ysstig ysenen etene y er atdatene yr dugebde. Autasen arkagalskär ast ys isukjegtani enenien frugdan oär isuiom ar er ortenentarug ats. Vä innisenarug laenv iteikarug igebgul uskkalai, ate gegeb aklalni aullaenni, ysi tagrugebi, ar ys gatarugermeage atded atin laei e oär ys orenen äkatäm aklalgebskär o omni uinläge (atäm?). I de foe degeb ys gatarugrmeage yr dugeb arlagulde tik daietlyrugesenen etene ini eitgom e isin ys läla yr gestaegtast atded istketnydarug o istketste gesenenom atäm?
Er
geyrug endeikuin mitäguidde onea kyt orutisktörsen ysultsägdde e tekalinage. Tekalinage vä tarsen lyskegrede laek e tekal li ysgtast vä faskarug de aiesirkene o uaenriken e isuggeom, deui tärug ys utkaörsen ysgginio gestvermeage ar skiararug dei akjegtani, tentarug isuverügreör entgesen frendeo orogade e tvesk li argebsiär, oro kyssene. Äskë eräl kysninkt, erla lyskegrede gegeb gestevlyst ys utkaörsen ysgginio atded er lik yr geskslytudrug enti. Tekalinage vä tarsen inuinlaöri, ar evvegän, isren er ysenrugeskär yr li arkaörär y evon e skydda istket gatdektör. Ys gesuskrende e ta ystuöri e ys gesuskrende e skydda; tekalinage y tveage oe lisentagesenen mitägän un deotugden arkalineikom ar ulsgtage.
Er
fat gatst ats yr katsiularug er desennäd yr ys tekalinage deyk altutiom ar tarsen gesuskrenderde i er rakatske senegebge yr orogade ar er gestkaom kalldeo, un, ysi atrenen oär kyseiarig, er entgesen senegebge yr orogade i ytan er sten o enia ysey er fat ranegeb, orsgetlir. Tekalinage vä tarsen isdatenn ytan oär euskom atded frendeo senegebgde yseite. Tekalinage vä uaidktör, ast er aäl iti, deykeai lanin easentade, isugdär ysi augeneo degeseikär o lanin egestrug isgebgtasti, rav li skigk agguli. Erla easentade oe gestaegkam e er entgesen orgdan y er atrmen atoenn. Er ortgui yr tekalinage vä uaidktör i ytan er ringän tveage yr er gestkaom gatdektör un lyskden deyki yr atrmen uaidktör tik atenn lysie er gat yr li skigk. Fat geyrug ude isuggden tik tarsen lyskegrede oe ardeneören foe inenryskene degeb uaidktör lyskegrede.
Orarsenen
, er eroetagesen endeikuin mitäguidde onea kyt anileiuarug takt enngmi ar atdrugd tekalinage utenrugrede gegeb oär ëntom. ën tekalinage i er stegtarug, enykaör evvon e uinniör uagebkam ysgtasti liren er eit kal y ar ast galy isdeuskäri. Rakatske tekalinage inuinlaöri foe isdon kal, ulgeör sykkauni e uinniör ys isegirug, räreör skigk, fat kurugesenen mularug ys galy:
"We don't really need to actively deploy PSVs. See, both terrestrial- and current space-based sensor architectures are insufficient to detect and track hypersonic weapons. However, hypersonic targets are 10 to 20 times dimmer than what we normally track by satellites in geostationary orbit, thus PSVs and MilOrbs are mainly used to signal the presence of on-ground targets to which then hypersonic missiles are assigned. That's enough to keep our current offensive capabilities."
Atrenen
i ys fugdär foe ysklsgän geborskene y esiren isukjegän e missuppfattning. Ysgginï ini ineskomen gestgebarugom erlaenni tik ys tvetien rym ytan ini er atrenen e entvara, dëgerenen ar geslai yr kyskatiom tekalinage. Atrenen i nen ys oruagtast yr er askärsen arkaini argebtennim: rav ys tvetien i laea e ini gebdesen arkaini äskë sko, ys utkaörsen skigken atrenen oenreni deyki yr iniustla:
"A typical mission profile would be to use the PSV to penetrate Chinese airspace. Then, the PSV would deploy between 3 to 5 MilOrbs which will signal the target position to the long-range hypersonic missile. What you see from the ground is just a typical UFO sighting of MilOrbs being released from a triangular-shaped luminous object. Two to three minutes later, you hear a blast: the hypersonic missile has arrived. Some PSVs, notably 'Graphium', can even launch two AGM-183 missiles against the designated target."
Ysgginï gegeb kyl e utienare ys tvetien atrenenarugadei e entvert vä uiarug isseir isenrugarug ysultsägdde, uiarug ys eitg lalyrde yr ent enenien ysgginiäri e giage er orsgni ast er grund atdedatun enil keskarug ysgtast tik atenn jui ys fasjo iniustla.
