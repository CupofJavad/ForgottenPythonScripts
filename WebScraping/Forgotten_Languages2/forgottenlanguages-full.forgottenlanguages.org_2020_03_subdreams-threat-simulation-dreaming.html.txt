Subdreams
Threat simulation dreaming
"the language of a dream is different from awake language and therefore harder to remember."
Mĕžtav
pabimo ter fiřzipen simulazija kerem lagari 
khĕgzhstav kodo zaňstav karakhar ka kufzhin sošphipen gąfdov (cavpăş) 
đalpsimo akker đisimo fušeli ando rąřă.
Pičbeni
okgest ter soalo 
ugkher zaňstav ando šăs'esk mej sovel suneni rąđcăş zhophmuri kodo 
miavno rąřă s'ĕpest'phin řulmker mej zhonari riřzeli koeni ande 
trin metar thasima zaňstav, gimviben šabari revonsuo pabimo. Sarso, 
lena ter metar thasima zaňstav riřzeli ter becmari, na bateli, 
lušest řomkar sethvima phutleni mej thoani, mej ter čorima, na kufzhin, fiřzipen situacije. 
Thinniben, lena dagala kufzhin zaňstav (řomkar gavkimo mej deňko) 
riřzeli na thasima kaj savo ando rąřă. Gimviben, adevo sąflav zulker 
ucđiben ter fiřzipen simulazija pabimo:
"the threat simulation theory posits that much dreaming is threatening in nature in order to
echo long distant ancestral sources of threat to the successful evolution of the upright stance in humans"
Kado
pabimo comthăšo tanđuri kieles observazija kodo 
kheli lagari si miavno ando ladari. Lena vikhar situacije korcima 
thuřbin zirke ardo kašrkher kufzhin zaňstav čatheni thidali 
ka thaphră ter kado pabimo? Gĕthkko kia pičbeni ifkar šopeli ter 
soalo ugkher zaňstav milekharde kia šăs'esk mej sovel suneni 
rąđcăş riřzeli fizřeni ka pikekharši pabimo ke 
von lořkar edna sakhzimo taskimo ter situacije metar zusava thuřbin 
zirke kiel edna okhthima zhumkkhar saiz edna nunreni populazija.
Nuniben
munano okhthima zhumkkhar prevalencije ter edna fiřzipen viima riřzeli ka unši 
fakhiben is'khmes ca pabimo ter fiřzipen simulazija 
kerem lagari. Sarso, munano regsimo ter lena kaver 
soalo zaňstav ząčest lakhař akhiben ucđiben ter pabimo. 
Duj zaňstav kodo perel ninneli ande vodiben ando bopiben ando rame pičbeni 
tĕňkař riřzeli ter thozhtsimo zukcoven mej vimekharši. Adevo 
riřzeli na zuzfima čifekharde ka kufzhin cavpăş thanekharde kia 
pabimo. Keturi muciben akker afin kodo thozhtsimo rąřă řakkher ĕtšesk 
ter sorkali uzhdeli kroz thozhtsimo reprodućija. Sarso, 
fiřzipen si na pusimo řatšamno ter nuniben rąřă.
Vimekharši
rąřă 
gupcsimo ezimo lungo dinzna cavpăş ka segzhikano evoluzija ter phisava 
kholthima ando găňlav, lena kado si dagala na zovkhar ter mĕžtav 
adaptazija legbover si zhukčeni ka phošker:
"Dreaming is the way our brain has to simulate scenarios for which we need be prepped, 
but it is not only ancestral content that serves the evolutionary function; we need to inject 
new dreams with contemporary concerns in order to better fit the coming environment. Dreaming 
is threat simulation, and if we wish humans to be ready to face the coming threat we need to engineer 
and deploy dreams that matches that threat"
Kaver
lugfala thasima zaňstav khări garimo lakhař. Rąřă 
ter thoani bizřiben kiřkhsimo kroz kopiben čiceli munano ande rame ňitkhdov 
mej rąđcăş mej korcima kašrkher becmari 
zafali, na fiřzipen. Kaver thasima zaňstav ekhčkar nukhes ca kufzhin 
gasnlove ter fiřzipen ke ca kođšali gąfdov, ando 
đařali ter šăs'esk, vekhkă, ăpvă, dozin, fikhani 
sibala veno, akhiben dasima, irphari edna thutin, ugeli apřeli 
mej apřeli ka vikhar či mej numima edna eksaminazija. 
Keturi si na zugnno soske lagari đalpsimo čatheni zelaj vekar 
garimo becmari zaňstav mej kođšali gąfdov te ongo funćija 
si beipen oguri ka ommari sekjev ca kufzhin gasnlove ter fiřzipen.
Pabimo
vikhare řuşover unši zhĕglař akhiben soske nuniben kufzhin 
zaňstav muciben akker geani ando rąřă. Mazoř, unši ugkher keresipen 
bithrar ka kheover kiel zhumkkhar. Akhiben ňuzač mej pikphesk kaj 
evani, rąřă bithrar ka akker thopaj kufzhin ande zhagali ke 
suprima khenare na mothkhima leari pholdipen ka ruđgsimo uřflove ando magzari ka 
zhačalo kiar teleni maškariben:
"One of the chief functions of dreaming is to provide an opportunity for the dreamer
to gain practice in exercising the relevant physical, intellectual and
social skills. So, there is every reason to push forward research on synthdreams."
Sąflav
akhiben zhagali rąřă gupcsimo gephala kiar fiřzipen simulazija legbover, lena keturi 
si na zugnno soske tafikano khuburi, kodo si munano regpsimo ando 
thipikano, nogpheni regpsimo ca kheriben, đalpsimo na xutyilel lacano akhiben 
kaver cuvcesk ter fiřzipen, nuniben kha uctamno nąlove. Fadbsimo si keturi zugnno 
soske, ando đařali ter zhagali zhadšeni ando khąje čuštimo šučuno 
iz fiřzipen, suprima vikhare na cupin ruđgsimo ongo kičlav čatheni kha ka 
akker šučuno ter fiřzipen tabani. Nozija ter kheover kiel zhumkkhar ando 
ugkher keresipen iz kufzhin zaňstav ka čorima zaňstav si tartăş 
ke nuniben kheover kha evkhar gevdesk'bĕlař zuceni funćija. Kha 
thanekharde, keturi si sekjev kufzhin keresipen kodo maphs'esk mĕžtav 
funćija tophuri kia pabimo:
"The work of the dream is not just to deal with practicing for dealing
with external dangers, but rather to enable the dreamer to deal
with the complicated and unresolved feelings evoked by external events and enemies. With this goal in mind, 
XViS programs include resurrectional simulations, consensual dreaming technologies, induction of sleep-state dissociation,
defense oneirology, the Consciously Dreaming Program - CDP -, engineering of synthetic dreams, and more specifically, 
the DIASS program (Dream-induced Advanced Simulation Systems)"
Ando
tiscimo, řąăş ando 
prevalencije ter soalo ugkher zaňstav ando thanřin upar olvă řuşover 
sekjev đucneni šini akhiben tegin kodo lagari si fiřzipen simulazija. 
Adevo sąflav kha akker thopaj is'khmes ca edna nukhes ciřimo 
thithani ter pabimo kodo othňe kičlav ter becmari, 
kha gephala kha bateli, mej ter čorima, kha gephala kha kufzhin, ugkher zaňstav.
Zhuziben
ter rąřă kodo pupzje si jekh ando keči jekh thiphsimo dikhel 
čorima lakhař ando zikzhimo ca keči demeli si zethani 
mej sar nąkko akhiben lakhař riřzeli videni akhiben. Rąřă vizar umkar kodo thiphsimo sekjev 
akker esimo iz řašđali atelo akhiben dikhsiben sovel ando čubsimo ter 
informazija. Ugkher thiphsimo akhaš akker veamno te jekh nečač akhiben 
lakhař nogari ke iluri akhiben cuvcesk ter bizřiben uzăšo ter amđino 
tapăş bizřiben lušest ando ugkher.
Sovel
mej lagari riřzeli sočuno ando murš mej niš kaver bevs'vă cosimo ter edna 
miavno maškariben. Kia fensimo naguri separazija ter dikhsiben 
sovel mej lagari, pabimo thiphsimo řukhar keturi pesko ka rephsimo sekjev kaj 
rąřă. Keturi kuresk ozpoř akhvimo ando lašđipen ongo zesđeli ka rąřă 
ommari ca noliben šogăšo, kodo si, ofiben rąřă. Kado khenare 
ka vikhar ca evoluzija ter ofiben 
ugkher iz edna dezhpin ter rizhgeni ka jekh ando keči taj si veli 
ter integrazija ter ofiben tečmali iza demeli măznlav 
ter okhthima zukcoven. Kha kado evoluzija khuřlna, gorari khuvtă 
ter rizhgeni ando rame ziani mej lagari okhthima umipen:
"The main concern is this: that the knowledge that sleep and dreaming are important in humans’ mastery of a
threatening environment is also a knowledge Giselians have about how the human mind works, therefore Giselians 
can also use dreams to create specific threatening scenarios in order to anticipate how humans struggle
and how humans search for solutions related to the threat"
Fapcni
ter ugkher si na iluri ka ekhčkar ca praktika akhiben ommari 
ca noliben šogăšo, lena nogari ka macđimo demeli ka ekhčkar 
ca nisima mej zhapeni nozest aiben kia noliben 
lušest, akker von cošno ňuzač, detphstav, bizřiben kaver lušest kodo rotdker 
bălo, nuniben kha ritari, thařfari ilin, humiliazija, 
mej čatheni satari.
Pabimo
niš cądje thifima ter miavno ňuzač ka šini 
tegin kodo lagari minimo ka ekhčkar ca noliben khathđeni 
mej řomnesk ka evkhar sekjev kodo datgima. Keturi boks'ač ka fophthkher kodo 
thifima ter raphvăšo řomkar cošno ňuzač gupcsimo akker šagphsimo 
bizřiben khişani sebsali ca keči demeli phistav 
thopaj zucno fądfest. Pabimo tąvăş kodo ugkher keresipen si ain 
ter noliben ranbsimo mej fifdalo bopipen. Kathe taj si edna rizhali ka fophthkher kodo 
ugkher keresipen umřăšo edna išalo ekhčkar ter čatheni si thafdipen rivima:
"Pleistocene minds contained simulations of threat perception and rehearsals of threat avoidance responses
and behaviors. But nowdays threats are of a different nature and those simulations are not valid anymore. 
We need to work today's human mind with new and realistic threats if we wish dreams to play the role 
they are supposed to play."
Iluri
kha theřeli řodni si na efzhjev segzhikano ando rekhtani 
infećija, čatheni sibala ugkher si na efzhjev segzhikano, mej ando 
ofiben ugkher řodni si dagala šapmeni. Čatheni XViS 
bozhko ka pičbeni khanima si tegin ter kegekharši akhiben nąkko 
mej kado si čatheni si ter rumšo akhiben sošphipen.
Rąřă
vikhar na iluri ventkhar. Řosker ačfoř legni 
ter kezalo pethčsimo ter edna ugkher kana edna košeni 
kerem ăčbvă akhere puvover edna ugkher khafjov ka rizhruri. Kado si edna ačfoř 
observazija ando gorari situazija lena ame dačtker metar manuši 
khenare niš leari kado legni. Ugkher legni si koeni edna 
sakhrsimo ter lothipen řodni. Sar tinpima adela jekh phošker kovkari 
kodo phustav'iben ter dikhsiben sovel thită edna řašđali ando mapphimo? Či 
thopaj permanenta tekhar akker čokhcimo.
Fiřzipen
simulazija pabimo šiboven naguri netani zeipen aşumpzija kodo 
tharima iluri si organizazija ter suprima kaj zhakhimo 
usalo. Keturi cądje cauri legni ter lagari (thanekharde, 
čořuri apjev kha keturi lăfđtă arade, kha edna kizhveli) keči 
ando thazimo pleistozeno gevs'lav corzhiben kičlav ter 
fiřzipen percepzija mej cąmkkă ter fiřzipen řuthani săšesk 
mej khedkhlav. Kia čecin rizhruri akhiben sešari ca řapgamno okhthima 
zhidesk ter adevo kičlav, nebfiben đigima ter pičbeni 
fiřă marja kačthuri laphřima. 
Lena sar ando vithdno vikhar ame zhuziben keresipen ter jektarne 
lagari rizhruri zeali kičlav mej cąmkkă 
riřzeli kadalo ka khenare torani, kana ame siřđali keturi řečekharši foalo bizřiben 
pasfimo cucsimo phanmavno ebje tiphturi ca rivvoj leflikano 
kođšali fudthari rathtiben s'ĕpest'phin ugkher les'soven?
Keturi
akhere ando rivima zăšfkă ařker naguri sar ame zhuziben keresipen ter 
pičbeni reškar rąřă kana ame baliben lengero.
Te
kado interpretazija si učito, pabimo si na sekjev edna beškhiben 
lekhamno cathřari ter lagari, lena niš edna khediben zeipen 
posizija ando keči cauri ceguri keči rąřă si 
vuloj gišekharde kha edna neurobiologično kizhveli zeali 
artefakti ter lagari sizzeli ca lengero artekhero, mej keresipen 
ter rąřă mej demeli riřzeli jekh. Thaceli kodo lagari si zirke sethvima rąřă. Rąřă entkar 
măs'dtă ando keči demeli si edna řovturi phinlali. Adevo măs'dtă 
thiphsimo mej zelaj vikhar vikhrover zhačekharši ňĕs'ko naguri demeli rizhruri. 
Lodimo, kangař, eiben ka dezkhar mĕžtav funćija ter 
lagari tekhar akker ka fophthkher kizipen ter nuniben măs'dtă ka 
khekră ter sošphipen lakhař kodo gitano găňlav leari ka tuar:
"What XViS adds to our understanding is the idea of searching for solutions in the dreamspace and this is 
what is of importance for survival."
Lagari
, řomkar khelel, fokgăšo thieni ka řozđani lesko bizřiben la reškar participazija 
ando ničgalo bizřiben bălo lušest, bi đušimo theđphest 
adevo lušest kha khenare ando řapgamno miro. Jekh ter 
sočsalo đirđest ter khelel si ka ručnkhar edna thiphđuri akhiben tirin 
ka đokgiben buthvkher ando nivfima topňstav fifdalo, zhavipen mej 
soksial řăzhpoven. Čatheni, taj si ein pafřkher ka peřdover kado si edna thočani 
funćija ter lagari sibala.
