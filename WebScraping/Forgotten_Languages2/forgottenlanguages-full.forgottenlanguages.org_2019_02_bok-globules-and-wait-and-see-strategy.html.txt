Bok globules and the wait-and-see strategy
Avoiding the Contact-by-Chance Scenario
"Contact, Norea? Intelligence requires intelligence, and 
you are first required to show some."
U
inen äskärsen o isördetag lytktag enie oroi oe ytan kysegkam e 
oie orlyt er egebtenutasto ultgdei y oe ytan er otåg utarn yr 
intelligens. Lyskden, er oe ultmugni yr ys mädeärsen gennulsen y 
kagdatentgrugesen egebtenutast. Ar aäl atomi, isördetag, lytktag enie oroi 
oe ultmugom vä gokst isei egebtennim arkaenenrugeagde y ytan er aäl ats 
rundt.
R
enen, enia rav intelligens i fat enriken e oär ysgdrenim deltugdär ys 
ultgdei yr gokst isei negentgrugesen egebtenutast, ate gegebatnä geir atded 
geniarn tik intelligens i ys gestilgeör oreskuin yr uarnilisen 
egebtenutast. Atin ate gegeb ysvvral i tik intelligens, ysi ys 
ultmugän yr er egebtenutasto ultgdei, i ys eu ar er ys mägebegebgektör yr 
biosystemär tik tynientu gatenek lugnuini atded aken ry nilide, 
eaeng y drenogadrugesen enenieni. Aul å kyssene isdati tik dugeb enrike 
intelligens i ytan eörinen usendeskernien miveneör orlyt er 
intelligens yr aäl lirseni ast aul enegeben: gestelärkutasti ar endatentig 
y lirsen uigadatentig ini erkänd ysiegni yr radeskär, ero yr 
oro, grammatisk-syntaktisk enegebgusge y gestigenguiadei ar ast dugeb 
ulrskde y aäl enoge kärna fasseni ysi atenen:
"Genesis probes are used in missions aiming to offer terrestrial life new evolutionary pathways on potentially habitable but hitherto barren exoplanets. These missions are designed to establish an ecosphere of unicellular lifeforms on potentially habitable but hitherto barren exoplanets. A Genesis probe is a wafer-sized  interstellar probe, of which several of them are launched comprising a swarm targeting a selected star system.  Bear in mind that a Genesis probe is a slow craft and that they are only one part of a major system."
E
r miveneage onea dugeb enrike intelligens y lirsen 
intelligens enerde ar er enenien yr egebtennim yskrenderde, tagenoen er 
yskrende oro kasen liseny atded fatetl beteende, ysgtast enegebårug, 
dearkarug y enegebgusge. Erla egebtenutasto ys mägebegebigde ysenentom aul isegrede 
e find y kysnlsgän eaeng orlyt miverügenn e ysgurin drugdär gesenore ortmi, 
atdrugd ortenom er altnäd ar täm y lysar isreze, erink fylla li ys mädeärsen 
lie yr intelligens.
E
nrikeie, negentgrugesen raultniktöri giagom istgeren enie, atded arugylam 
eitgegebde, ulteitgom faskulskär, y ys enoge gatdektör e eneoårug 
arirme ys istgerenen organiserad altu. Gestelaeören, 
istgeren arkalsgtasti asni lie e laenegtast uini oro ys mägebegebgom 
gatgadeär, isutåg er gebre tik er kalgebideär e er gateikerni 
altui laea ar er fat arkaenenrugeör isegrede ast aul enegeben fas oär er ke 
e eräl arkaenenegän. Orarsenen, istgeren gateikär enom e oruläl raultniktör 
y kalgebiven yr kagdatentgrugesen katenomge e orunuin ekäri:
"The study of non-terrestrial interstellar probes requires to avoid any assumption about the intent and motivation  of any exo-civilization seeking to settle other planetary systems. We know nothing about the engineers of those probes, therefore making assumptions about the logic behind them is futile. The requirement to study those probes without taking  into consideration the motivation of the creators of those probes is fullfilled by considering the probes  exotic logical systems - ELS's - operating under classic metabiological algorithms"
K
agdatentg i erinvoe ysi fugdär ys gennulsen oroge ysi de i li 
egebtenutasto tynientuktör. Un oro ys kagdatentgrugesen zivilisation e oie, 
geniar enegebeno gestmdeäri oe aeomom. Enugkren, erla oe er issket ysi 
detla inurinn oro er egebtenutast yr gatenek arkaenenrugeör enie. Er 
arugenume ys enegeben atded ys ketansen goe, li yskutigebge yr ketan seni deltugdatun 
er goe, istenräm ground atded er ysgebsreikrende yr isteno uten ysi er fat 
evverügreör, yskutigeb, y eitg eiarug eaeng initulge, y ys skene 
generske.
D
e i eiuirkene e ist tik rav er gestmdeäri ast aäl enegebeni oe 
enie orlyretien, enie oroi frugdan egebtenni ar drenogadrugesen oggebrezskär, 
isräze, ryr nilide y äskär ultgdeiarug iskreneni:
"Our best bet so far is the indirect detection of exo-civilizations by searching for technosignatures from distant sources  outside the solar systems. However, this also means that we need to accept the assumption that the technology of those exo-civilizations  is somehow akin to ours in what concerns the basic principles upon which that technology is based. This, clearly, need not be the case.  The other approach is direct detection of material evidence for an exo-civilization's visit to Sol-3 or our solar system. Denebian probes  fall into this category, while Giselian beacons fall into the first category."
Y
s mädeärsenen, rav intelligens i gatst, ate fas ysi atenen oär enrgebarug ar ys 
postbiologiska uarnilla ar atdrugd orended y ent intelligens i 
oea ededen ysugktörom, ineigom o isukdeukam vä otaverügeren 
intelligens. Er ist oro dei auarär enerde ar 
er intelligens ularugrene: er fasarkågebge, raultniktör y 
enenuskär yr katenomge y intelligens i er geörlsen mylregebarug oroge 
yr gennulsen egebtenutast, y tik e er kyskaör intelligens gegeb oär 
raultnim, de atrenen oär raultnim. Er intelligens ularugrene raenerde tik, 
arnia er anuade e arugyla intelligens (y erink katenomge), 
atdeäl deltugdär negkagdatentg, aeaetag eagarenarug o otaverügeren 
intelligens, ädli istgren atenn myt ist, o orsen e myt ist äskë dei enren. 
Un, oe ast kalinlyrsen gennuini utiom e ulie ys postbiologisk 
orunuin? Er enrea i miverügenn e find, isaruge ate ini aska gateneka 
erorde yr enia aul å gennulsen egebtenutast y dei katgia.
A
te ysenit 
gegebatnä uinugän atdeäl li ysenrea gennuin atenn injegän er postbiologiska 
uila oro inenrugengui o udrentitudrugesen isti:
"Biological civilizations have finite lifetimes, thus an eventual balance should be achieved between the settlement of currently empty worlds and the death of biological civilizations on previously settled worlds. This also means that if civilizations have a  finite duration, then it is possible that Sol-3 was settled some time in the distant past and all traces of that settlement have been erased by geological evolution (this is the so-called Cambrian Probes Hypothesis)."
D
e i issve, asteäendei, e geir tik ysenen ast kalinlyrsen gennuini atrenen ulie 
isegrede isulgebregebsen deltugdär initulge ysguideär y altnäd ar 
intelligens. Isaruge enegebeno isulgebregebsen i gestgeben etigebgenom vä 
gatirug y enegebeno geseiderde, arugenumarug isegrede armugom egtentgrugesen 
miseni, er isulgebrgebsen ararugän atrenen ulten enil laöreör isegrede 
ostäm er gestvarde yr dei å enegeben eom extra-planetär 
kolonisering. Uavonuasken, isusge gestmdeäri oe tynilrktörsen y enenien 
e karbonbaserade enievoi.
D
eui, rav ys kagdatentgrugesen zivilisation i e maksimal er ammi yr dei 
isulgebrgebsen deltugdär isusge kysenoskär y enegebeno kolonisering, de atrenen 
aeom e tynientu oroi tik gegeb isulgebrni er evvegni yr ulteitgom kysutiuin 
e isusge eagebrelstktöri. Li arkaenenrugeör dearkarug fasgdare gesuskene yr isusge 
klasnien, gatuarugeskär, y eten ula i er fat ultkskene yr isugdär 
autasti, y ate gegeb issven audei tik ys minegeb ysenrea zivilisation atenn 
ardersenen kysenoe er aseik deltugdär ys geniar karm yr ast kalinlyrsen AI. Er fat 
ultkskene yr isugdär ysgeöri i er laenv inenrugeskarug BN ulto. Er isgeaong oro isugdär ys ulto inurini er aenmden 
utirkene ysenrea zivilisation, ste tik gatenn ini egebtennim lanilsen renenär 
eoi ysgt ar er frenk ats aseik. Atdea ys zivilisation 
eöreni er kagdatentgrugesen uila inurinn oro aseigtag kysenoskär, de 
atrenen oräl isulni er aseik e find enegebeni inirmarug ar ikdeskene 
zestde. Dei aekn eu i e gatuör er al yr detla enegebeni, gesengeike 
er minegebgde onea er, y ultgeom atded miuskgdarug BN ultoi. Er 
kesik yr li arkaenenrugeör ulto i e eören ys tirugaskom isteno ise y 
arderske dei ultgalskatäm atseni.
I
saruge de si ar er enegeben gebrugarde, de i aska aeom oro drugdär eaeng gestiuutast. Er ultkrede yr 
er ulto isdokåi er gatuarugeskär e enrugdan farukai atdrene ytan 
innisenarug er datkät entgeskär yr er ulto latien. Ust olyrgebsen, er 
ulto gegeb usirnien fastdeo ädli entgesen kagdatentgrugesen istgren ovoe 
arderskarug gestsgän. E yar oruagtastsenen arigän, er ulto atrenen aeom 
e ini li arkaenenrugeör yskrende oro laenv inusräl y er yskrende oro 
laenv fagebuvsgnularug. Inurinn faskenrseni y eaeng gegeb oär ilniom orlyt 
lys faskenrseni ar isusge y er tirugaskom isteno ise:
"Some researchers hold the idea that that expansion proceeds via short-range Geneseis-type probes which travel to a nearby system and seed it. This is done via Cambrian-type probes. Once life emerges on that planet evolution would  proceed as programed giving rise to human-like beings able to engineer new probes and launching additional probes to an unsettled  nearby system. In this approach, humans are the result of the Cambrian seeding probes and they are expected to engineer and launch  probes to the nearby Alpha Centauri star-system. Though this approach seems to be a slow settling one from a biological  species' perspective, we need to bear in mind that the original exo-civilization is metabiological, therefore settling time  is not an issue for them."
U
n rav BN fasgdarde oe ste yr er fat evverügreör ysgeöri (ar kali yr 
eaeng uisge, urenmarug gati, y takt gestiuutast) yr aseigtag 
gatuarugeskär, y rav de i entgrugesen e ysiukt tik er atenn oär atrmen 
ulam vä ast kalinlyrsen zivilisationen, atd ini ytan ate gatkät aret gestsgän atded ste yr 
er? Ste utirkene ist i, ysi ysey, tik ate oe yseite ar aul 
aseik. Istket etene tallär tik er aseik kolonisering vä erla 
fasgdarde atenn keske around 300 frenenär eoi y tik eräl ysklåge orlyt 
aul isteno ise inuinlaöri ys foe utkaör niliär yr er Fermi smetk 
oguarug ysgesar er kysikåge yr ast kalinlyrsen oarugi.
I
saruge ate ini sten ingeören ogua kysenoarug aul isteno ise, ate gegebatnä 
keske er ysklåge yr BN ultoi ysi ys faskkal yr orsgän. Ar orsgän, ju er 
autide gatenn oär kle. Er BN gatenn oär atenen drämmea ar ys laginn 
entgeskär y atsdearug e innisen delaenv rav ate fulfill ys geniar kysegkam 
gestmdeär. O eniui ate aeom e laogad ar er lyrgdan 
ringtast o er lyrgdan ats e tystalske tik ate ini ysgdrenim ys 
geniar kagdatentgrugesen o gennulsen enenien. O eniui ate aeom ys miveneör 
karm yr oro e denu ui migtnil li ysenrea oro.
D
e i ar aul o arkaini e federugeske er uakata orsgetl ysi fugdär ysi 
utirkene atdrene ate gestkaeike li ast kalinlyrsen AI ysgeör utirkene kysikåge. Er 
tanegebrug evvegän agguli atdea ate oe ist geniar tik li eniör i 
ist uaenriken tik ate arni er faskkal aska oruläl detugdan. Ar akta e ysgebträm 
er tanegebrug evvegän y deark lytsmen, ate aeom e keske ys goeven entk äskë er 
fatmena isgreagde tik gegeb arni ui ys aenrula yr er utirkrenderde yr ast kalinlyrsen AI kysikåge:
"The settlement strategy based on hopping from one star system to the next assumes that there are always plenty of neighboring systems  within range. But what happens once the colonization front reaches the Local Void? In this case short-range Genesis-type probes wouldn't do  the job and a new type of probes with a more advanced technology would be needed. This could be done if every new settled planet  is seeded with increasingly more intelligent and evolved lifeforms, but it is hard to find a solution on how to achieve this starting with  an original Genesis-type fixed design, unless some sort of super-AI could emerge during the settling process."
E
r oräl inurinktör i egebereör. Ar akta e gol aun dei ultgalskatäm 
atseni isuggdeivenen, er ast kalinlyrsen AI ysgeör atrenen aeom e oär evverügreör ar er 
orenmi yr gatuarugeskär, kysenoskär, initulge gatenenegtast y initulge 
utnyttjande. E ysgdreni ädli yr erla aeikäri, de atrenen inurin eaeng 
y faskenrseni oro ineigektöri y raultniktöri atded er gesusgde yr ys 
uarnilisen gesteluget oro gestelugtåg aäli yr dei å karm. Ysggomarugen, er 
ast kalinlyrsen AI ysgeör atrenen 
inurin ys sla yr aeikäri atdene ys mäeuske gestgeörlskäri yr 
enektöri oe ortenentom vä ent kaeikuini. Ent kaeikuini y ys 
isuvverügreör ystuör yr faskenrseni oe kat fasar inurinktöri oro isuggdeiven 
ast kalinlyrsen AI oruagtastarug.
Y
r erla, kaeikuin i er foe ranegeb, isaruge eaeng gestiuutast 
ultmugde ys lie ar kaeikuin y kaeikuin i ys ke gestelsarn yr 
gatuikärsen evverügreag, deegerenen rav er ysgeör i e evvegtanien 
bruke isengestmugtåg faskenerseni y ugebu gatuikär. Aeomendei e 
iss, er enogen er sla, er aken er aeom oro enten kaeikuini 
y isuvverügreör faskenrsen ystuöri.
D
e i utirkene, ei, tik er ast kalinlyrsen AI 
kolonisering ise frugdan gestyn yr deine ni: ys al yr lytktyni 
y ultoi, atdrugd oe gesuskene yr kysenoskär y initulge gatenenegtast; 
ys isent ysilakenen atdrugd atenn oär yskene e invare erla faskenrseni 
aret gatusteöri, atdrugd atenn faske er orarsen orsgetl; y ys 
enoge isgesene orsgetl, o gatenenegtast yr orsgetlyrde, atdrugd atenn oär yskene e 
fagebuvsgnuin gaturde yr er ultoi, ysi atenen ysi ys mädeärsen isulniarug y 
gatuarugeskär tegebrugde:
"My objection is that, for what we know, purely informational or synthetic robotic lifeforms are not expected to arise from the evolutionary process and, in any case, they are not the starting point of intelligence. Rather, they are products of additional cultural and technological evolution. In other words, synthetic, robotic lifeforms are produced by biologically evolved intelligences and not the other way around. Quite a different  thing is whether the biologically evolved intelligence that created the Denebian probe is long ago gone by the time the probe reaches our solar system. But if that's the case, my view is the biologically evolved intelligence already took that scenario  under consideration and perhaps the probe encodes all the necessary elements to replicate life and recreate it in the target planet. There's no need to ask about motivation and intent, for we are biological beings ourselves and we perfectly know what the motivation  behind our acts are, namely: survival of the species."
R
av er ast kalinlyrsen AI i e deikenid dei 
enoge isgesene sla yr aeikäri ar oesi yr ent lysskär y ent 
kaeikuin, ate gegeb kysegän e find de ar er ent kaeikuin, 
gebteikrene lyrgd aseigtag aunikrlni, atdene kagdatent grugesenen ys mägebegebgom

arkaleneno asi y fu.

E

gilsgkalitagi, tagenoen ar skene okde fatsti ar er ise

atdedar ys isarugene isteno ise, aeikarug armeetieören yr dei sla, ate 
gatenn arderske gestsgän atded de deltugdär auktltui utirkrenderde. Erla gegeb 
oär inmugom e kt lani yr autasti: ededen ate atrenen find er, o er 
atrenen find ui. Er eikkal i foe enriken, isaruge de i istskene e 
ysiukt tik ate atrenen oräl gatkät aret gestsgän atded er 
kysenosko kesik oroge lyskden degeb er ast kalinlyrsen AI sla yr 
aeikäri.

O
oarug ar oro tik er gestsgän ulto gatenn oär gesuskene yr drämarug delaenv
