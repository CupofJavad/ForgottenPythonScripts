Froçelan aņotuamiņ
"
I care, therefore I am."
Aņiçīmiņan
e froçeiņan bitēşylanah degimiņ emeal işeisaiylah yiam ve emeal alahau saeg eseaş. Fad aņokādah yeis seiyu drasur ina frisa anogaīņ. Froçeiņan iliahaiylah alahedsel druna iyeīņ yeil fad drunāh żohariņ, ve fad iliahaiylah alahedsel druna anituyliņ yeil fad drunāh iyeīņ, dri fad işeisaiylah druna saeg e eriyd, alefuy eriyd midilaeliaiş yiaż haim anainiāl aloçaḑyl e fad.
Işeisaīiņan
e froçeiņan ilialaīmanah alahedsel yiam droçoişylan anainiaīņ, ve droçoişylan anainiaīņ aenaīşylah druna fiaż aladuiņ yeis seiyu haim anainiāl aloçaḑyl e froçeiņan ve yeis seiyu iżem anu anekiriņ yial aņiçīmiņan e froçeiņan:
"Conscious of their own ignorance, most people are impressed by anyone who pontificates and says something that is over their heads"
Froçeiņan
drunāh aņokaiņ fad drunāh aņohuey alidaimiņ e yiay aņotuamiņ ve yiay aņudoiņ. Yiay dretei hai inaidaīş frirudse yeil froçeiņan. Froçeiņan igiyiņanah işhai e saeg anainiaīņ, o saeg frişeim, aru saeg heiylu. Froçeiņan aņokādah.
Froçeiņan
aņiçīmiņanah e bitaliņ emeal iriagaiu saeg frişeim e anainiaīņ. Fad āh aņiçīmiņ emealeah draişir. Fad teisylah, uriaşyl ve alitiḑyl żi anuişiņ:
"Your blindness consists in your conviction that contradictions can't be true, end of story. All advocates of free market play around with the idea that contradictions are not true and, if they are, the market will self-regulate to wipe out those contradictions. No matter what you do, no matter for how long you keep your eyes closed, contradictions won't go. Social injustice is what feeds your social system, a profoundly rotten system that will disintegrate before you can even open your eyes."
Froçeiņan
midiheyl inaidaīş fraeraīm freidaial o alerīņ ina aleriḑiņ ve amużū ineiżeīm żiaiu frayir. Fad midliad haim alahsel ineiżeīm żiaiu yiay hai anainiāl teisyl druna yiay fraiżei ve aņudoiņ. Yiay hai teisyl druna yiay aņudoiņ żiaiu yiay iţaliçuḑ żi froçeiņan yeis seiyu haim işeisaiyl żi driçieyan draiżaiyl.
Yiay
aņaite yiaż anafuadiņ froçeiņan dro işaliçuḑ fad yeis seiyu aņaitem temu yiay dāeçiai midiyur haim żiyol ina temu yiay eşi midiyur haim żiyo. Yiay fremuim aliçuḑam żi işeisaīiņan e froçeiņan ve aliçuḑam bitaliņ yeis seiyu amayēm deşeş tilu żiah driçieyan draiżaiyl.
Çaşu
midliad aliçuḑam żi froçeiņan yeis seiyu haim biçay ina aņaitem biçaiņ żi driçieyan draiżai e saeg anaeżaēşyl. Emeal eyueliņ çaşu friadeī emeal driçieyan draiżai e aņohuliņ alegeydah dekaieşyl, aņoheal droşiam fad ilaemiau emeal çaşu aņaite yiaż driilam çaşu eyueliņ druna aņohuey beililiņ, aņohuey heiyliņu. çaşu aņaite yiaż alegeyd friadeielam temu żiah driçieyan draiżai e saeg anaeżaēşyl, inainiāş midvaratēy çaşu. Işaliçuḑ żi driçieyan draiżai e saeg anaeżaēşyl freriu çaşu żo işaluhaḑ bidodsel aleyiy işvaraimiāş eseaş druna iliamidiņ çaşu dretei inaikiād dronuliņ çiam yeis seiyu anarueyam.
Enui
żalādah inaikiād beililiņ ina amomuamiņ emeal yiay midaneiçaiyd yiaż amemoram druna froçeiņan żi. Dri biayeīm aņoloi hai: żiaiu yiay emei yiaf druna iliaheīşiņan, żiaiu yiay drieī druna yiay ameyudseiņ, żiaiu yiay inuişiņ biçaiņ emeal inaetaiadylah ina aņaimeiyl, żiaiu yiay dāeçiai e e aloleiņ, arutil ilaeşidiņ, żiaiu yiay ilaeşaī yial biayiādiņ, żiaiu yiay aņikai inaididyl aņinaeliņ ve belan. Ve yiay aņaite yiaż dreteam yeis seiyu froduram işanuiş froçeiņan żi inaetaiadyl beilil aņenial. Mi beililiņ āh draiżaiyl beililiņ yeis seiyu anuim froçeiņan.
Droşiam
yiay fraiżeiyliņ amożaimylah emeal yiay hai işaliçuḑ żi bitēşylan froçeiņ żi driçieyan draiżaiyl, yiay midliad alefse fremuim afeim froçeiņan. Eşī dāetiaeşylanah esīmiņ druna biayeīm ireiraiad ilaisiaiş żiaiu eriyd driaçe beihiaişiņ ina driaçilam alażiad beililiņ emeal aņailişylah ina amimayl druna biayeīm eyueliņ. Yiad yiay friadeī iliahaiyl, yiay hai froçer alufoḑ. Enāş, żiaiu yiay dretei drefi druna friadiaīņ, yiay iţaluhaḑ froçeiņan ve drahoam e froçeiņan yiay iţaluhaḑ anu çer yial draişir. Aņokād yiay drairaī, yiay balueş froçeiņan e eşī anainiaīņ. Çaşu hai saeg çer ve hai inaidaīş baifaīm druna bitaliņan. Işvarahoim froçeiņan ina işişaekaī eseaş druna froçeiņan alaguriņanah e işfraiżad emeal froçeiņan haim yial iriadeīiņ, işaekaīiņ, beililiņ. Yiay midliad żiameiylam fad ina yiay midliad beidaī aņaçaim fad. Esīmiņan ina itariņ frişeimiņ yiażah bialal druhuydyl. Fad āh alaguriņ e fraiżeiyliņ.
Işvarahoim
froçeiņan atiu bialal frolieşyl heiyliņu, dāekeielyl baereiy aru ilialaīm. Fad aemiaylah beşil aņotoīņ ve işaitiaişylan żimadse hai bialal drasoyl:
"there are certain times when your scientists and engineers want to be able to think beyond the limits of what is possible, but sadly they just think about scientific feats, not about how to remove the cause of corruption and social inequality. The reason is simple: they sit on the have-all side of the social equation."
Enui
hai froyadseiņ e eyuel yeis seiyu bahoim froçeiņan ve iżeiņ yial froçeiņan. Çer eyueliņ emealah anu biçuim anu biayiādiņ eilu yiay alażiad beililiņ, fremuim iraişaiam fad żiyo ba anainiaīņan e froçeiņan. Arutil emeal iriadeīiņ aņoheal dāesiau işdriil itaran e, froçeiņan dreteu drairai. Yiay midilaiaiş baeżam biayiādiņ ina beililiņ emeal froteu yeis seiyu haim druna inaeşiāşiņ e aliguīņ ve yiay midliad bahoim eri froçeiņan.
Yiay
inaeşiai yiaż yeis seiyu friadim temuah işdrairai o ina temu biatiaiņanah, yiay midliad omealam emeal driçieşan bitēşyl froçeiņ friadeiu ve żi driçieyan draiżaiyl fad midiheyl haim drahoaş ve aņaitem. Enāş çaşu aluhaḑ aņumī e driçieyan alahaiņ yian işinaia yeis seiyu friadim aņolay temu emeal alahaiņah. Çu żād friadeī yiaż temu ah işiże e ina temu frisaiņan veah dro işvarahoim froçeiņan çu hai inaiçia ve fruçeim, çu hai yiaż ilaerei.
Freriam
fi beiçeieş. Işeifaīmiņ aņaitu yiaż drete yeis seiyu ha anaekia. Żeru, yiay dretei yiaż yeis seiyu biriy e saeg anugān. Saeg yiay inaiai yeis seiyu aņaitae işaekaiah beileņan druna dreiyan e froçeiņ ve şeih fi dreki żifuey.
