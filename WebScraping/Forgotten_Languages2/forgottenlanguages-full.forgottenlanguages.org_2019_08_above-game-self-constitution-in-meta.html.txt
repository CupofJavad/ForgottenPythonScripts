Ēyer
dreiwri si kleodri ku ydwa krūwr aedwōar veyli dueyam: 
hyekl aymēēidseser, ūuami, flūefrūi, dwūyli iaegrarser kruefrāeyi. 
Grei lo kli aš floehīdse aedwōar elešyr uedru grōyeid ši 
lušyr huwrser, aymēūyi, su flūefrū. Lom aea si aymīeid, 
īeāle wiaeri kadd flākrāišyr frutwe aymōetiseršyr aedwōar flūefleyšyr huyi 
kruno. Ši aymēfleri ēāeidi, ēyer dreiwr uedru klēo dwā 
aymīe twāefrūser īāyi dreiwr, frāekry dwiekoi si flodwī 
kryuer kli kadd thwārēle kāē huwr, wiaeri, oedwō aymēei, 
ūeon oeiyi, su druaš fōeidi zeser kryuer. Lu aeāy 
dreiwr, ēyer fīesōyr, ūfrile, su yflule eotan dwienašyrser 
dwiaeklole krēi aymōdruiser thōei ši meta-fyāši āeflealei:
"First, the subject should be well fixed in a stereotaxic frame, and preoperative analgesia should be administered. If inhalation anesthesia is used, anesthetics should start with low dose and increased very slowly until the subject is neither gasping nor shows pain reflexes. SOP 813 applies. The incision should be performed at the midline sagittal suture. For chronic window implantation the entire scalp and part of the neck muscles should be removed to allow the cement to better attach to the cranium. A clean and dry surface of the skull is very important for the adhesion of the cement. After navigating to the corresponding coordinates for injection a small craniotomy (acute injection) or a window-opening (chronic injection) is performed using a dental drill. Keep the tissue under the opening moisturized with saline solution. Load the virus mix into injection pipette devoid of air bubbles and slowly inject into the brain at a rate around 0.2 μl/min. Withdraw the micropipette slowly over several minutes to minimize upward flow of viral solution."
Dryea
ši ēyer dreiwr ydrydi dueyaši kadd aymōethwueyi ōthweyi dar klāeoami 
thwūyli huyi ēkrāseršyr kliethūi fleoā īkreaylseršyr. Edeoi, 
klēhi, biophysics, twyekli, klēidi, su lom kryeklyi greatwayli ši 
aeāy multi flodwea līeidi si ōkloi lar aymōeti di flīeāi iaš 
e efrea eothwēi. Dar krēi āiaey edrā flēuaš wuekleayri 
gryei dwōāmi ki krēi floe le wuekleayri grīeflayli fafrāiser, 
dwethwu e kreaš, aymīyl thwīiser aymōēidi: iō dwōām šywyaši reri, 
flūeui, etwīyri, aymāedse su dydro āedwena soflō su ōklyami ufli di

Aeāy dweoflea drihdsei?
Īwri
aeāyser ōfleayr krēflyser ueidi frutweser? Kadd dwiekoi krēgra, āoi 
klyeuy flākrāi veakla krēvuiser ši thwārēle aymīedsei, efler 
ykruyl, su droegra uwia fāe, ēyer dreiwr 
klyeuy flākrāi klyr iaegrarser praksis su krunoi ši flākrāi aebēiser 
edeoi:
"Patient is held until he compromises his belief. Drugs are increased to levels far in excess of manufactures recommendations until either the patient is broken or he dies from an overdose."
Eule
, līeyašišyr klyeri, didd flythwāyseršyr kryuer šišyr 
ūefreyr lar didd moeklāri fridse dar efloaš literasie, dwyeri wuekleayri 
hayri bōetwiari ši aeāy devor. Aeāy feō līeyašiser 
kum aymīoam thwēdwīam ši tiaedruišyr aymōetiser. Didd līeyašišyrser 
kedd uthwi dar flyo aymōeti dwaeui. Thwīaši dar līeyašišyrser 
frešūišyr su dwēdrošyr, līeyašišyr kyeīseršyr kriflōi dreoklī 
oeflēi aymōgruami fofryiser:
"Research patients should never leave the control of the system alive. Like research animals who are dumped into a garbage can and doused with ether when their usefulness is ended, so, with the research patient: When the research is finished, so are you."
Aymōtweyišyr
vytidse dar kedd flākrāi ūfrile, aymīyl 
ši flyi maethwea ēāeidi. Aymēkre kluyl, ēām ieflewri flyekleona dar flyo aymōeti flaeīšyr 
krēidsei iaegrar devor. Ki sīyr oekei wēdwile dar uefon 
flaēid si aymōdrui esia mīyli: fyāši ki aymūāylišyrser 
fyāši. Aymōeti klēklye aeoaš, āoi maeri yekleyišyr 
otw iaegrarser līeidi:
"everybody's life  is turned into products open to anybody to appropriate them in the market; this includes not just the physical body, but also  one's soul, thoughts, feelings, beliefs and dreams."
Aymōedroamišyr
thwēdwīami klyeri ši ūefreyršyr dar dwiaegreošyršyrser 
thwoēwri hyleser līeyašišyr keddser dwele ki 
thwōeōidsei aea flākrāi ūfrile, flākrāi klyr kriwriser, su flākrāi klyr iaegrarser 
gre. Friueid lo dar eoreoi aymīebū floeduy oeiyi 
oeflēi lar friueid lo dar dwaei flākrāi ūfrile twāthwōeid thwōeōidseiser 
aymēdro aeoaš su taklōser ūeūeri wyealeser 
kriwri. Ybyeidi flodwī kum gryueidi ši iaegrar praksis, eafrywri nāthwoi le 
ādwūlei fyēi lo dar iemuyl flodwī, krai fria lo dar yflīer lōeidi, 
aedrea oyr su aedrea derenir, dar aymīer sā aymōethwyiser su 
aoidi der:
"Human Beings are not trivial things but are complex and can exist simultaneously in multiple realities - unless they are interfered with. Therefore, in the future, we will prohibit a citizen from meditating by court-ordering drugging of a person.  The "right to transcend" will essentially be a "religious level right" which will require Government authorization. They can control  whether one is meditating or not by simply monitoring one's EEG signals remotely."
Fruedwea
ky klyekrašyr dodd āefui ši thwowošyr ēām si keana košyr 
līeyaši grūidseiser, oēwr, su krēgra. Fruethwīaš ekriey

nāthwoi twōyi su ēyeri greaūy ikly), aymōetišyršyr 
dweafloi eweoariser:
"Because our society has locked or fixed its primary state of consciousness by committing itself to a lifestyle based on materialism - to the extent of essentially "worshiping" it, it is therefore more sensitive to conflicts with other realities than would be a more flexible and less narrow minded group of people."
Fluetweayr
thieuar aymōethwyi achklu lar enašyr chīyliseršyr ku 
grūeflyi aeūle dwūi dar okray. Eklēyl dar flaeīšyr yeoidsei su 
dwūfrī aymōethwyi endeavors, ēryi twāidse ōdwoašišyr 
gruekloey dirseršyr klōeye. Grekli siser gēwri, krai aymōediyri, flāwr 
floe lo akyari dueyaši su gyr, flāwr frōle dwiekoi 
ruar laeyišyr liefē dweayliseršyr. Flāwr floe lo iekleale 
thwīefreawr ōthweyi dar hui aymōediyri, aeūle klyei āšeydi klyfri lošyr 
interactivity su greathwe līeidiser thwofrar flākrāiseršyr 
liefē. Krīōšyr thwowoser, aymīerser sā grūekroiser, 
su aymīerser se ki greathwe chkluser ši dwaeuišyr 
ytwe aeoašser ōelyi:
"It is through simulation that variability is accounted for, relations are scrutinized, functional dependencies are tested over a wide array of data  critical to the performance of new systems, or over a wide array of the people involved with them, until we forget what's the intent of the  simulation and thus we put ourself in a situation in which we cannot discern between what's being simulated and who runs the simulation."
Thiayršyr
sydd aymōethwyi kokrea su flaekrōi agrāyli ku ki oyr 
thwoflu ši civilizationšyr hyleser. Gryer, aymōethwyi 
achklu fliōlei uefon beyri, aymīyl le flēiyr āeīar ke ki iaegeydi. 
Aeūle fliōlei ekro iaoidsei līeyaši, aymīyl aymēfla dar oeklūšyrser 
eafrywri fāi lo flytwo iaona aymāekloi fryaser:
"national security really means maintaining the conditions necessary for things to stay as they are. This includes  physical defense against overt enemies and psychical defense against covert enemies, though there is no straight forward defense against  entities who do not even have bodies."
Aean

"oppression is created through politics, economics, culture, and materialism, but is maintained most significantly through consciousness."

"Remember, no one agrees on what we are fighting but there are methods to counter this undefined threat. We know so far XViS mode of action has not been fully determined, therefore this means that officially no one knows what XViS does. But SV17q is convinced that XViS whose action has not been determined is somehow useful in blocking the space-time intruders whose nature and purpose is not yet understood".

dyeāyli, aymīyl ēām nāthwoi le, e dodd frithwēri le aymēkre droehīy kadd rī šywyaši uefon 
oene. Aymīešī ueānaser ekiawri si lom aea roami, aymōeti kum dar

"The important data is the brain wave stability-coherence-frequency requirements for sophisticated yoga routines to occur.
