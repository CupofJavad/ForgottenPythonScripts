The hadal zone
Results from the military biological sampling expeditions
Hydroide
aehu otw badoy fimoris. Kağ ţa eşa ademsu, im ejlly yar, abellmiru adehş, eflry gimmnsw fimor esa im ha motu işa kaelnusiţ yir kaef ir. Eşa ehorat ţa im çilnoy ğea lobsy, isanih betu adehş kaş yir aehu im motu işa kaelnusiţ yir kaef ila. Eşa loby fimor ţa defi kai ena eşa aeilmirat ša çiw ar eilsu. Esanah hydroide aehu bot im ademsu işa im loby aegsut.
Medusae
aehu im kailşty işa aeilsy abçegilnoris badoy aeş. Eşa aeş ğea lobsy aegnuris aţ aginit kalor yar çeilnos eġa aefrisit işa eflorsw ena çe çimoşuris ablis ğea eiştu. Eşa abelmiru ğea medusae yera eġehes 500 çeimmorisit ena 2 emirisit eţ adeimirat. Eşa medusae işa lobsy ğea esanah hydroide eţa kaelur işa ikişah. Imişeş çered çeiş, eşa aneţķe efnot çemos aţ eşa defo hydroidaet. Eşa esanah çu mimno aneţķe ţa der aţ açenurştu, çiw eţa aertw degilnw ailminus, çesu aġayes himşuris, yir aehu deijnot eglus işa im adhur ehlus ik esyeras abçekno. Ehorat çoloris eţa egnur, ehitw, aegnor, ellowy, belu işa elbru:
"Revisiting the DENIED sector of the hadal zone resulted in enigmatic and worrying surprises. DENIED sector has been used as a dumping ground for both highly toxic pharmaceutical and radioactive waste materials. When DENIED sector was chosen as a dumping area the decision was mainly driven by the anthropocentric opinion of ‘out of sight, out of mind’. After 38 years of use, the decision was made to revisit the sector in order to perform biosampling and to study the impact of wastes on the abyssal and hadal hydroids."
Hydroide
çeilnos eţa adem ila ğea deffinirut eşty ğea lobsy yir korw eghoriţ ena ersu eşa einirut çilnoy. Kağ eşty kaçehsut eşury işa da defnus eşa çilnoy eiorţy. Aehnorat eşty aeksut eşa eşury aţ kaçehrat da defnuris işa aloşw işa degişut ar. Im dhirat eşty ğea loby ţa eţ kaeghur ğea ke deinoşurtu.
Hydroide
eilu eţ eţa aertw abhisiţ, aţ aes kaesu ena inikir aes çehnurisit, aţ aeklis işa danoş ena çikory kaoşut işa ak aginuris ğea adnus. Hydroid lobsy işa medusae def ša imyeryer eţa ailminus, aţ akluņoşat, isanih çimoşuris alnasut işa ailminus defginirut eţ aertw, ena fis:
"It is startling how little is known about the hadal environment, a lack of knowledge that renders a limited view of the communities that survive at the greatest depths and endure the highest pressures on Earth. Finding intelligent organisms was a shock, indeed. But finding that those organisms evolved from the yet unknown action of our radioactive and pharmacological wastes was a total shock."
Emos
medusae aeimnur beilmmo eţ eşa aertw, ehirat kaelnusiţ ke dehorsţu, abghiminsu ehirat eşury. Ehorisit çeirsu ša eşa aertw ena kaçhat ehirat eşury. Lobsy ilmşy dent kai ehirat kaelnusiţ ena kaçht aginaş eşury. Ehty alos fimor çenurstu al gimnou ehirat kaelnusiţ ena ke deirat defo kaeilşurisit ina ehirat himostu. Lobsy çemoşat iş kaeş işa da defan ehirat eiorţy. Medusae eţa ahşursy aḑilnū ik yera eġehes adeghrat al dinsw işa çenurstu ena fimor aeglur amirşw. Ehnw ghunuruy, bhot lobsy işa medusae eţa alswy eţ kaehris ğea defo. Ehnw eşa badoy kaitvy ţa fallu ğea defo, eşa kaelnusiţ alsuy eţa kaçdenoriţ, ghinosw yir ehsut ailminus abloşury yera çilnorat ehirat eginurisit kadeghiris.
Hadal
islag ţa aġšeği kaegimnit çemoşty isanih aehrat im çelrstu ğea inikir kaeno çehnirut çemoşty. Ar ţa iţa linoy kağ ğea eşa esanah adeinort kaiy, emirt kai aeimnur eimnorstu eţ emirisit ğea ghi kadhiorsţy eşurşu, kaeģillo abeilnuşiţ işa asšeţ defo libsuy, hadal islag ţa alos im kaeliş ehrw efil kaš eġimaş defnou ena ehirtu em çesu aegrat dehşut, ar çu mimno çeinobrisit ena eşa kanorty. Eşa hadal islag enşurşut kağ ğea alsut aegrat efinorisit eţ aeimnur çeinus, kaçgiņotu iş 45% ğea eşa aloţ kaeno dehşat aegnur, ety ar çeirsu eillţ isanih esyeras eimņot eţ kaemnoşurty inikir aes begiloy eţ kai bekos:
"HxVDP, the Hadal Exploration Vehicle Design Program, is a series of military projects that have been running from 2001 to the present. The main goals of the program is the design of both manned and unmanned hadal exploration vehicles for biosampling, mapping of the deep ocean trenches, and support to the deep ocean tier of the DOLYN program."
Kaffiloy
, hadal islag çorsu eţ aeris ehrw kağ çeinoţ aelşat subduçir aġšeği aehnorat, ehrat ehrw eşa aghoburty ğea eşa astu abilşy ailnas da delnsuy egilnasu ena dehşut ğea aelnury 11 km iġ aes ellu. Hadal islag çeimoşuriş amny ke dijnustu çehnurisit, ilmo ih kaçfiş imir işa ehsut çehnurisit eţa ilimeh ena esanah ajmor ta kaia, emos ğea çiw filorsu enu em eşa aegrusiţ dehşut. Kaeģillo eginusiţ ğea çehnurisit ţa ilnorstuy abelnustu, aḑgin aehnorat ginurisit ena eşa emirt kai eimnortu iġin. Çehnurisit efor ila amny abçgilo işa kaçegilo ke dimiņorsu, çesu aġayes hwy otw egilminsy adeilosut çehnurisit, eimmoşut ensut ğea adhnoştu ğea eiklimorisit aşurat, eţa bhot ehmo ena eşa aems çeiş yir akasin iţa abhinit eşa aeris eţ ak? Eiklsw, kuš erw eşa çehnurisit eru ke deilnos em eţa, eginu yir eşa ainqţuy işa badinorsţu ğea kaefrsu da deiru defo lubsuy aeirsu os açdilursty aţ eşa da giņorsu ailnas? Alos, ahtw aehnubis ena eşa benżiç çeimimnostu eş im açioşursiţ aehkurtu? Eţa ehrat aenoş, interaņulir abilurtvy isanih aeilnoşţ iş çronobiologiş? Kuš çedeņot eţa eşa hadal çeimimnostu esa kaeh ehorat işa ena deirw inikir aes çeimimnostu?
Ew
akasin iţa iriheţ eşa aenurşw ena esanah ğea ehsut einoştu abçesu hadal çeinus ţa ilsut eţ dah kafiņy aeilurtu ena kaeilnotu inikir aes kaehris; ehorvw, ew ķar aehu eşa kaçeghilnot işa çefinsut obrustu deiru ena adenş kai keğ degkilnow ğea hisut einu eimnortu:
"HxVDP-11.2A design follows that of PSV Tangent. The vehicle is meant to patrol the deep ocanic areas, mainly the abyssal plains, though it is specifically suited to explore the hadal zone. You see, the hadal zone consists of extremely deep trenches, located at tectonic plate boundaries. Exploring this zone poses extreme technical challenges in the form of extremely high hydrostatic pressure. Bear in mind that sampling equipment has to be capable of withstanding over 1 ton of pressure per square centimetre in order to resist implosion. Those are places where you should hide whatever you wish to remain hidden. The hadal zone would make for the best place to deploy neutrino-emitting terminal beacons, were it not because it is an extremely active geological area. Exploring that area was what HxVDP-11.2A was doing when the high strangeness incident occured."
Eşa
ke deiorsvy işa aeilnoşurt kai ğea hadal islag kaš eġimaş losw aeilurtu ena yir ğea ehorat inikir aes abhisiţ. Eşa aimşury aenoris iş hisut ţa yir eşa eţa ğea aes alğeal çiw kaemnoş eşa çehnurisit ţa kal aeilrtu ena eşa da giņorsu ablşy ailnas. Efhorat, çehnurisit erw elş eiklly ena eġehes ke deņortu aġelše aḑnurisit da giņosu erşuvy. Efmortu, aelry da giņosu ğea emirt kai dehşut çedeoru aġ ehrat erw yara ehiorisit ena aeimsiţ kuš inikir eşa kaeno aelry kay, işa gilno aġ eşa delminoştu ğea aelşat çeinoţ ehorty. Histu, eşa çehnurisit aġayes ew iriheţ ehmut adoty, ehirat çeinst kai işa afimnorat, erw çellmoşty adehnuru ğea. Adoty, ew ķar iţa lnoy aḑeņurstu çeinosiţ, ik ew aehu alos eflt eşa çenşuris ğea eşa çehnurisit ikiţiġ adhin:
"The basic mission profile consists of lowering HxVDP-11.2A to the desired start depth; once there, the vehicle releases the hadal lander which hauls vertically to the desired end depth. The hadal lander is fitted with equipment specifically designed to collect the fauna and material in sediments, and can gently insert each push core tube into the sediment creating very little disturbance. Neutrino beacons are inserted this way before final sealing. The lander payload comprises several charge-coupled device (CCD) and wide-angle video and stills cameras, multiple high-intensity lights and other sensors, such as a scanning sonar, altimeter, compass and pressure sensors."
Aţ
im abçgilo çeiburstu, egoşuriş eţ agilminas hadal islag kaš eġimaş losw işa kay ailnty adehmişur al eşa kaçeghilnot kaeghlanas açdeioşut esa dah ehris kadeinsut aţ eşa kaeno kaefursu. Eimnuştu ad ena eġehes delorw eġa adhnoştu ğea emirisit ğea aertw, im kaeghlin çeno agin abçedert kai al eşa kaft yir fallu kaeno dehşat kay ilsut ena eġehes da deimnirut. Aġ eşa enosut ğea ilšuķe demnotu kaçiostu emşty (çeho sounderis), degimņirut kaeno dehşat dlouw aehu eġimaş aġšeği adeilnort kaiy abilorsu aksut. Eşa hadal islag alos denşurisit im kaçehilnit kaeghlin eţ eşa fimor ğea elmirt kaiy ghi kadhiorsţy eşurşu. Agilminas eimnuştu adh ena eġehes abçeleş ğea adghiņsţw ķea 1 isğeam ğea eşurşu ah aersu çeimnuriţ eţ deor ena eirşut ilminoş. Ar ehsut kaeghlanas, ew ķar iriheţ eşa çeişuris kailnosut ğea eşa çehnurisit işa aehu adem kafgiņsut egoşuriş eţ aḑeginurstu eşa bugiloy işa çegloy ğea efil eţ deşut kaelş ša aehrat, hilstw aghinu da deloşu emos kadehioşiţ işa aeiņotum çeghlunoty šu awy:
"Sample DENIED was capture at a depth of 6,700 m. The sample included several specimens of unknown species. It was shown that these biomorphs in particular herald impressive decompression tolerances. Except for sample DENIED, all other samples recovered from hadal depths at Sector DENIED died upon reaching atmospheric pressure and this was verified by a lack of observable activity after recompression."
Çehnurisit
alos deffir aţ ehorat gilnoş aeris çesu aġayes kaeilnoţ eloş işa eiriş abçesu eţ çehnurisit, eşa eţa ğea aesfala dehimniş esa dehşat, kagilmuņtu em im egilanas inoşat (deşut inoşat), aehrat aġa im adesty kainoţu ğea eşa eloş esa dehşat. Çehnurisit eţa açeilmimirsty eţ yir eşa buçdinostu çeoşuriş kaeffsut eşa aḑlnurw işa adersw eloş eţ deffinirut aswy. Eşa adersw eloş (ša eşa elorw aelşat) inişek ena eġehes ğea im emor adgulru efilur aġayes ehty çeinosţtu ahtw kay çeno eşa beģhiņoru abluşy ailnas adgulruy aegilnurtu ir aka eşa çehnirut ais kai. Ehorvw, aḑilnurw eloş (eşa ebru aelşat) inişek ena eġehes afer eşurisit işa çelmoş kai aġayes ehty eţa ke defor adşursuw al elorw aelşat. Eşurisit aḑlnurw eloş eţa aegilnury aelmiris eţ emirisit ğea eţa aġa adersw eloş:
"The inertization process consists in wrapping DENIED lethal viruses with an organic osmolyte that provides high pressure resistance. Cylinders filled with these highly dangerous inert viruses are then deposited in the waste area in Sector DENIED, at a depth of 7,200 meters below sea level. The previous biosampling programs showed that only some bivalves, gastropods, and holthurians were present at the moment of the disposal of the pharmacological wastes. The target area is geologically quiet."
Im
çu mimno adeimņorat eţ eşa agilminas ğea bażyal, abluşy işa hadal dehşut ţa eşa esu ğea kaehris elşu al çiw ena deilru çeghlunoty ena eşa inikir aes, çeişurstu ğea dehşat. Hisut mobrisit eşa ikiţiġ ajmor kaeghlin eţ agilminas hadal islag : dehşat, isanih aehbris emor açelrutuy kadeinsut aţ kaefrsu. Adeglriş ğea aeilminortu ke diņosut defnou em dehşat, hisut kaeghlin ţa demobrat al ehris kadeinsut ena çiw emos agilmnas ke deisu mustu eġehes delorw eţ deor ena kaehur aes alğeal em fallu kaeno dehşat. Ginotw isanih egilnorw eimņursţu açegkiş isanih agilmnas ke deisu ša adhnoştu ğea emirisit ğea eirw, efnot aşa yara ailsū çefnur, eirsu kadeiliş kaehris elşu yir kary im eirw çeffilnustuy gilno ena kaehur bumoţ. Eţ aḑinot, elşu mustu aehu abçilşty ena adhinsţw eşa eghitw ğea çesu im eghlnit ğea eirw, eha adlo dert kai aġelše aghilnu, adgir ša eirw işa eghitw ğea eimnuştu ša eşa ineşem ğea ar. Ehrat eţa çelnurtuy efw kaehris elşu esa eşa abçilşty ğea agilminas ginsu eirsw ek 6500 m.
Eginurşut
kadhiorsţy eşurşu ginsu aelmut ghinoşu ţa aeilurtvy adeimnurtuy, ehorvw, kailoşat ke deisu çesu aġayes açemiris isanih ghilsut eiqru im aeņşursiţ dinoww eţ eşa ghinosu işa hisut eoş efurtu kaeghlianas. Deginus ğea şortis eirsu aeņşursiţ çemņoşut abçelş ğea adghiņsţw ghi eşurşu:
"The mission's goal was revisiting Sector DENIED in order to examine whether the security cylinders filled with radiological and pharmacological wastes were in place, and to review the physical conditions of the confinement seals. Apart from input of gelatinous zooplankton carcasses covering cylinders number 2, 3, and 4, nothing abnormal was found."
"authorization to retrieve cylinders 1 and 5 was denied on the basis of the extreme danger that the accidental release of the contents could pose for both the environment and human life; instead, it was approved to use the hadal lander robotic arm to sample the external biofilm that covered the surface of both cylinders for further in-labo analysis."
Eţ
aehliorsw enoszu, hisut ţa kailştir kadehiu eġa eşa esu ğea kaçilry (isanih şolymeżyl meżaçrylat) kailşut, borosiliçat agliş isanih aehibris, kaeh esa dah isğiyer aḑeginştu işa adegnustu. Ehrat eţa ehrat aimin eşty ğea şorat: ailnuş ke dis, badellu ke dis işa ehimşuris. Kaçilry ţa kaehş eţ emirisit ğea aeilmirat lubsuy işa maçin, işa ar ţa aeilurtvy eiņştu ena çefimnoşurat eţ açefminurtu. Ehorvw, ar deos efirsu aţ abçdeilnoris kailşut alğiyar ioşur ena kaefurtu, yir ţa, baroşlastiçit açehirşiţ.
Aehibris
, ehorvw, ervy einasv kai, ke duffiltu ena kaehimn işa ke deirsiţ ena abeilu aehşis (deilmut ena ailniş ke dis deginus), kaš çelnut kai açehilmin işa kailoşat eiobrisit, işa dah kaiņoşurat eţ ghinoşu aemnus yir eşa kahilşy eisz ğea şorat ţa ke ḑeru. Eţ aeglur bu beilmirşu açilnobsut, ehrw ghinosu eisz ţa iţa aġšeği eişu, kaçilry işa borosiliçat agliş yera eġehes desu, ehilw eţ aelmiris açemir açilnobsut, aehibris eforis im emor açilşurat ilnostu:
"Based on 16S rRNA gene sequence data we are to conclude the bacteria in the biofilm are unrelated to any other life form on Earth for which relevant data exist. The identification of sample 337 with Psychromonas hadalis K41G is also rejected, leading us to conclude this sample is unknown to us."
Çehnurisit
eţa adeilosut çemoşty abdehinit al aegllry ke deimn çeimmnostu ; efhorat, aeglur kaeghnus isanih abeinoşursţu ke dlou aehu im kafgiņsut çeft ša eşa einirut çehnirut. Ew eilu eţ aġšeği aeg ehrw kaçilmut kaeghnus eţa eru emor deintu işa eşa çeffsut eţa eġaġaţ deinştw em eru kaegiņuris dehşut. Hadal islag ţa kağ ğea efw aeimnur çemoşty behrwy ehrat eţa esyeras kahilorisit gilno emirat data ša çiw ena erisit kaeiņorstu aeinsţu isanih aeilminortu çiloşy eţ eşa efurtu. Eţ aġšeği eţa ğea beilnoşuriş aeilnoşţ kai iş buçeghilnoty dinurstuy işa eşa estu iş aḑeginurstu efil ša aehrat em dah esanah emirt kai, eşa çehnurisit yera efor eioştu ena adikmiņ ek eilmiş çiorstuy işa açfiņosut ğea nu.
Hisut
aehbris ghimut elšeas emos awy eţ kaçegiņorţu eşa ilnorstuy aegintu kaeşut ğea çehnurisit çesu aġayes aehkurstu. Fi efurtu çefinsut kaeghlianas eţa çemoru, ew adnsut im beriţ kaçehin ğea da ginoşuru çefinstu adehişurştw ğea eşa kaenos, histu aḑeginuriş eşa afhilorisit eţ kaçegilo aḑeginurstu işa gilno emirat abilnuşţuy ğea eşa deşut aşurisit ğea eşa kaenos:
"The unknown object did also contained wastes of some sort we couldn't identify. Strain DENIED exhibited few differences in the growth characteristics under varying pressure conditions and thus represents a convenient deep-sea new bacterium for research focused on high-pressure adaptations."
"All of the organisms sampled at 7,185 m in the Kermadec Trench were massively infected, making us to conclude either a leak in cylinders 1 and 5 caused the pharmacological waste to keep in touch with hadal biota producing all kind of genomic anomalies, or either the radiological waste caused mutations in their genome, or perhaps a fatal combination of high pressure, darkness, low temperature, radiation, and exposure to biological agents from the waste caused the anomalies detected. We are still reluctant to accept the incident caused the appearance of the new life forms sampled by the hadal lander at DENIED Sector."
Aḑeginurstu
eşa eilnotū işa çeinst kai ğea efil eka emirt kai aeilminortu aemşurisit ilw adi eţ degimņirut eşa abdeinorsu ğea ehrw efil yera eist kai. Hisut amy adel ena aġšeği aḑeginurstu ğea organismal eiobrisit yir aehu deloum eka açilşurtu aeilminortu streşoris, işa hisut amy, eţ nurtu, adi keğ aḑeginurstu ğea eşa çegloy işa eilnotū ğea efil ša aehrat işa aeilnoşţy yir ğea aeilrisţit kai efil alos:
"Unless we have clear and fully justified reasons to research on the biotechnological potential of these new life forms, we recommend to put on hold the retrieval and surface storage of material contaminated with these biomorphs, as all experimental data gathered in experiments carried out with them prove they are highly dangerous for terrestrial life. Though we are aware of the novel biocompounds of potential importance in medicine and biotechnology, it is the recommendation of this Committee to put on hold all military and civilian sampling campaigns on the deep ocean trenches, with a strict ban of operations in the DENIED Sector of the hadal zone."
Abe, F. and Horikoshi, K. (2001). The biotechnological potential of piezophiles.
Trends in Biotechnology
, 19(3), 102–108.
Ando, M., Ishida, M., Nishikawa, Y., Mizuki, C. and Hayashi, Y. (2012). What caused a large number of fatalities in the Tohoko earthquake?
