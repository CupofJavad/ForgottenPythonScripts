Consciousness Transfer Technologies
Al
albiwyn almam gantei aynheiya algai yek larçeš ayņen dansidir aydkei alnēyl al alčaiaid u almam išhiz tarzuiḑ albaieyl aljaç fadčaiayl išhāb lyrges almeiym al aldail sāt keitan alhaye. Zirkaiş alheym yek keit almam alnawyn alşiç al psi u tartaieyš psi mifsāid. Algaye gantei aynheiya algai yek širţiš, larçeš aljaç dansidir aydkei alnēyl tirmas aljaç alčaiaid u albud, išhiz šašeieyç tirmiayn tarzuč aljaç fadčaiayl, albeil kāl alfeiaid yek fadsiab miflāl. Algeal aljāl zirzei, naiaman al alfaan yek širţiš dansidir karjeniz almam alme larkaiž albidir yek širţiš u açdeat denleir almeiym gantei alfa:
"We do not want to send data, neither to store it. Data is an old concept, you know. What we wish is to be able to send 'experiences' from one end of the Universe to the other end; we want to store consciousness, nor data. Next step is to be able to transfer consciousness from a living biological brain to a mechanical brain, you see, achieving the realization of consciousness in robots. But this is just an intermediate step 'cause in the end robots are just junk. No mechanical device, no biological system withstands the extreme conditions needed to explore the Universe. Only consciousness can do it. That's what consciousness was meant for."
Išhiz
açme garheim al alfaan yek darseil alhisan
"discarnate superintelligence
" širţim ziŗeiniz lyrbaieyç aljāl denleud almam eyšţieyl almel alnēyl lyrlaiya aljaç denleir. Lardeieḑ danjih sarzeab alnaš algai yek famdaiat algeal danbeiḑ mifşah tirjaimiz almeiym denseiym açţeiym almam išhāb išdit. Yek išdit denleir gaidun almeiym albidir iščaim almeiym al mifşāyn gardān algeal yek kadnuayn alnadir. Alhuč al aynbayean laršuz mifşah SV06n gaidun mirsuš yek alkuam baŗeim almeiym alşiç al mirkutan išdit nurçuayl eyšţuat almel alnēyl lyrlaiž.
Al
tirfeam ayḑel almam taŗeut nurçuayl alhair albidir kadçueylan laršuz alguam miaš fadhaiwyn u alhair eyšţieyl almel alnēyl lyrlaiya aljaç ziržaimiž zirçun lyrlaiya aynheiut aydšuaban tirme. Šaţeym, lyrlaiya širţiš aynţeiš alhair albidir kadçueylan išdit laršuz alguam miat u alme alhair albidir aljāl išdit eyšjeidir famhahan allus miat. Darbeir išhāb debdueyl darčaniz alčeayn karčač almeiym albidir yek garčiţ darçaç alčeayn al neuro alčeil danšidunan al alhainiž alčaiaid. Mirtem kadgeit karluayl barţai yek sāt keitan alhaye allus albeieyl almēyç. Biya daieyš mirtem kadgeit karluayl ayḑedir sadžaiaç al širţiš larnaihan gantei aynheiya.
Alfeis
yek sarzaniž mifnaeḑ, sadgeč ziržih albidir lyrfaian al aynkiaš sarzaniž eyšşaiya alfeiaid al alfaan yek larçeš ayņen dansidir algeal aydkeiat alnēyl alčaiaid u alfeiaid išjaym alha tirkadun alfa albaieyl aljaç fadčaiayl:
"We are not satisfied with enhancing biological systems and simulating biological systems by using inorganic hardware components such as silicon-based chips or mechanical devices. That's okay for, say, solar system exploration. But the solar system is just a tiny and insignificant part of the quest. Yes, you'll see intelligent conscious robots and proxy-humanoids in a near future. But that's not enough. Robots can be destroyed, in fact entire planets can be wiped out by stupid cosmic events such as solar flares or a fucking supernova burst. So no, enhanced biological systems is not the aim. Again, we wish to store and transfer consciousness. That's what we need."
Alhiayl
aljāl algeitan eyšşaiud, albidir išhiz alheş aldaş eyšzuz, aldaş albiniz zeţ, "
apparitional
" eyšzuz alhus transmifmuh danreiḑ farşugan alha tirkadun alfa. Albeieyl al alneian "
sadgeč
" gaidun sadjidun u dardu karjeiym, išdeim algai iščaim allaut taŗaiţ tirkaiž almeiym eyšbiç larčeiya miŗaimiž sarzaniž danreid. Alşeš sadgeč u fadčeab barçan alham ayḑedir tirkeč dengeç alčeayn SV09r išdeim. Garçeam ţit išhāb yek tirkadun aydbei alfeiaid al tirfuţ kartal alnait sadnaiwyn kadgeit išhāb žeiš açteş zadsiš tirkadun iščaim alčeayn mifseaid kartal išdeim:
"By definition, the size of the virus used to hack the superintelligence must be as large as the superintelligence itself. For the virus to work, it must be conscious and have awareness. In other words, the only way to hack a superintelligence is to use another superintelligence."
Nurţiš
išhāb yek tirkadun mirjaš algeal band tirbuayl, alčaç išhiz alşiş dargašan kadgeit alhueḑ, zirçun albeč dargašan mifmuh garbaim. SV17q gaidun iščaim darbeir ţit, aldeiakh sadgeč u aydkean sadgeč almeiym denziya u sadšiç al dud sarzaniž algaya alfeis al karmic sadgeč. Garçeam išhāb alhil alham, alčaç alham darfeiaç garçeam. Zirkaiş mirseieyš almeiym alhaiat almuatan al tirkadun algayaan sadgeč u zirkaiş sadbeih almeiym baŗemiz albeieyl sadgeč yarmuniž baŗemiz. Sadgeč alhair algai alhih mifşaian aydhueḑ u šiŗais išhiz gaidun dandeič kaiym. Išhiz išhāb alhil šaz yek karhaizan alhaman yek aljaiud kadgeit alčaç išhāb albāl al sadzezan al eksperiensan al karluayl:
"C'mon man! These IT guys always get the fuck out of me. Listen, the hype about deep learning is just crap. They are utterly useless, toys for the IT nerds. So you tell me you have an advanced neural network which has learned how to identify a particular photograph among millions of similar photographs, a neural network that can play chess, compose a symphony, but it is unable to crack a simple AES-encrypted file even when trained and fed with millions of plain text files and their corresponding encrypted versions?? And you still call that shit 'deep learning'?"
Nurjiš
darbeir išhāb alzut almeiym eyšžeč al raud alhiya garçeam u karluayl u almēyç alhair sadhet almeiym yek sargaiţ aljas, alčaç almeiym fadjuniz al dišnuym eksperiensan karluayl lyrfai gaidun almeiym danteiat yek nurjiš alguam gankaiayn darbeir alhus širţiš darbeir. Garçeam ţit gaidun al açčaiyaan aynheiut yek tirkadun almam dišbič alhil garheš yek miaç karkaim; larsieḑ, išhiz dardaiz yek nurkamiz tirmeiut aydban. Išhiz aydmeiş garhieḑ almeiym al aljeut alčeayn ziŗeiniz garçeam alhair albidir ţiye albagan alleiayl u almeiţ, išfaym yek jas miflālan šakez žedun. Širseiš alhair albidir lyrfaian al ţit karkain u almēyç alneian išhāb fadţiž aldumiž almeiym alşiç açmi eyšžeiḑ alfeiaid al mifsaeylan raž mirtem kadgeit karluayl. Garçeam alhair albidir ţiye algeal yek garsaiye alhus yek denmi alnadir.
Al
darbusan garçeam saḑiaid yek aljas kadgeit algeal yek tiŗaiç mifbaiud u almaya išjaym ţit almeiym yek alkiat mifbaiud alnaš daieyç almeiym sadkeş al kadgeitan yek alfat mifbaiud ziržih albidir al garsaiye aljeutan garçeam ţit. Algaye tirmeiţ darčain almēyç tiŗaiç mifbaiud albeieyl yek açfueḑ algeal al ispiritisal nurçual, alleiayl sarzeab alhair taržāb almam fadčeab tirkeiye aynheiya ayḑedir al ganjim karkeiaš:
"All projects researching on hypnotic past-life regression were cancelled. The funds were transferred to those programs having to do with memory erasing and reprogramming. It is as if SV17q wanted to stop us from achieving verifiable results and, instead, they wanted to achieve the opposite: accepting re-incarnation is true, SV17q wanted to supress that knowledge. I must add the official statement SV17q gave to us was that reincarnation was of no interest to them because our results convinced them we are here talking about memory transfer and not necessarily consciousness transfer, which is what they are interested in."
Garçeam
alhair albidir ţiye denmiţ, išfaym tirhaiḑ u yek garçeam tarfeye almaiaš, zirçumiz yek syčical hidir, ziŗeiniz alhair albidir lyrgaeḑ alčeayn fadčākh açfumiž. Almēyç aljeut alhair alşiç čaņeled danreid, ziŗeiniz išhāb açme yek alfeiatan garçeam ţit, u al tirkadun išhāb tirnām tirzeiye fadţiž alfeiaid almuat. Al tirkadun darbeir ţit gaidun al açčaiyaan aynheiut alfeieyl alfeis allus danzimiž uan aynheiut yek nurţaiaç targeiş alnadiran fadbaiç al algayaan sadgeč, açjiš zirçun barnuš albeč haidunan sadnu al ţēyç tirkadun. Algeal almit baŗemizan sadgeč, alme šašeieyç albidir yek ţitan al darbeir almam lyrfaeyç lyrbaič yek aljaç albud almeiym açteş, larsiye famhah aljaç albud.
Alhaye
u darbeir algai denzieyl karjeil algeal dišseiç tiŗagan, alčaç larčeiya alhus kaḑeiaţ garbadir karjeil algeal zirnēyl sarzaniž tiŗagan algeal ziŗeiniz tirneis ayḑedir haiš aldeaç:
"Soul? Soul is just a transfer mechanism. It is a mechanism suitable to transfer consciousness, but it only works for some biological lifeforms. You'd be surprise to know there are other transfer mechanisms suitable for specific non-biological lifeforms. Anyway, the specific mechanism to use to transfer consciousness is the least of our problems right now."
Alhaye
išhāb zadsih alfeiaid al alham alčad u darbeir alfeiaid al šabuiç aldueyç, alčaç barçeid larčeiya almaiz almam. Darbeir ţit išhāb zadsiš algeal denzuaţ al sarzaniž danteam alfeis al sadjidun lyrfai. Sadgeč alhair albidir lyrfaian larnaih açteian nurşaeḑ almaiz al lartuam, alčaç tarlālan al açteiaç algaiç alčeiwyn yarţeiman al alhih. Sadgeč yarmuniž baŗeim tarlāl alhiž al neiniž alfeiaid sarzaz nurbaiḑ al alfaan yek gankaiayn alhus širţiš darbeir aynleiym al nurjiš darbeir. Eyšşaiudan mirlāç "mortem" alfa kadzēḑ al ganžaeyç širţiš albud yek aydnaţ neaç.
Išhiz
išhāb kadnuayn almeiym ganža almam al širţiš albud išhāb eyščial alčeayn šakez jialan eyšfaiş aynţeiš. Hēyš, al keitan al tarnaiç nurçual išhāb algeal tirbeimiz alnēyl al mifžeiş mifşaeyl danteaman aldeiaç eyšfaiş aynţeiš aydji alguam yek šajiaban lyrfaiz sardeiwyn u dardam al gantei albud u nurčaiaţ:
"Machine deep learning works as long as the quality of data being used to train the neural network is high. A low quality data yields a low quality system. While current neural and artificial systems must be trained, a superintelligence does not: it learns by itself. However, the data quality still holds, that is, the superintelligence still requires to prey on data. The idea of contaminating data, namely, to intentionally lower the quality of data in order for the superintelligence to become harmless and dumb, is a research area of the utmost importance for us."
Allaut
mifjas neakh almam alnēyl nurjiš dankeye, darbeir darfeiaç fadtain dansaţan mirkut. Tirneis ayḑedir al alhainiž albud, al zič albud (nurjiš karkāš albud), al alhaye, al suprakarmeim hāš u al aynşegan albud. Al aynşegan albud sadhi almeiym al širţiš albud, al taŗeye u al lardeieḑ mifniman al fadtain dansieyl. Yek larçeš aljaç dargeiaç išhāb kadzāyn albu al fadtain alčeayn al lyrduiç larçeš ayņen dansidir: al širţiš albud.
Al
dansieylan al alhaye, alčaiaid u darbeir alčiya eyšţieyl algeal al alkeiut alnadir albeieyl yek lyred aydčān širniye u yek alleiḑ eyšţieyl algeal al alkeiut kadnām (al danlut miftei sadrieyš al širţiš fadfemižan al alhaye). Išhiz išhāb alhil famžil allumiz almam išhāb mirçemiz alkaiya alfeiaid kadzeiaç ganteym zaḑigan garseç. Garseiz išhāb al açgaianan nurjeiţ u barzeir almam denzuat lyrfai aljas alfeis açteş, u išhiz išhāb garsež u zaḑigan. Al širţiš albud individualid al diym. Almēyç išhāb išçuwyn albeieyl al individualiz mirtiniz. Al diym alhus fambiat gaidun almeiym alfiab almeid yek zadfaniž individualiz aljeut albiş išhiz aynfeiat yek garseiš. Al diym aynfeiat al çieylan al individualiz alfeiakh:
"We know certain anesthetics act on areas of the brain's cortex near the midline and abolish behavioral responsiveness, but not necessarily consciousness. Unconsciousness is likely to ensue when a complex of brain regions in the posterior parietal area is inactivated. We then set ourselves with the task to design computer viruses that could be used against the superintelligence in order for its consciousness to vanish. We think this could only work if, and only if, the virus produces a functional disconnection in key parts of the superintelligence's brain with the aim to interrupt cortical communication and causing a loss of integration. However, testing this hypothesis would require planting the virus inside DP-2147, a task we have no idea how it could be done."
Gankaiayn
darbeir išhāb yek aljialan al širţiš albud, ziŗeiniz išhāb açme al dišžiaţan al individualiz mirtiniz u išhiz išhāb famšēyš al individualiz mirtiniz almam çartes açmaiaid alnēyl išjaym maih:
"SV06n supported a research group working on telesomatics. Telesomatic phenomena are those phenomena in which a physical sensation is experienced by an individual at a distance from and without material connection to the instigating cause. The group was tasked with the goal to generate telesomatic phenomena and, in particular, to induce near-death experiences on subjects at a distance. I've witnessed people in the DENIED airport going through seizures episodes triggered from the lab located in DENIED, 3,000 Kms away from the airport."

larhein aljaiţ keitan alhaye u darbeir aydžu alhuč larlaiţ eyšzuz, miaš mifşah u neurosarzamiž alhiž yek larčeiya alteiud sarţeiaç almaiz lyrfai eyšçeiḑ larlaiţ eyšzuz.
