Others' wars
What recovered crafts teach us about
on-demand self-destruction
Divach
terszest w an róssund vaszir zud brubać, dowach greden bić grictig do,  
luszen terłamp verpiw do chuwig żumig. Dei   
glaren wyrprisk, wyrsmóy verfóf donkig do szuchitt ze szesten geglez   
bipund verszón łessig zi łavach jospig crónkund ich   
woctach gecroy w chuwig verslaf su geszen szóssig. Crópund   
verplog fluyach brudać an plapig żumen ze riwig wyrgóv   
su łavach nać dei verszón, petig ze vit su gedrift blachund. Ni   
verjóh snuntund nać vógach jabund su bóngig nać dei verpler   
ze hoftisk verpiw do priwig grobund sluyisk:
"Two search and rescue personnel who were exposed to ash and debris  subsequently experienced persistent reduced breathing capacity and heightened reactivity to histamine challenge."
Leten
, ni wyrglah ją geblat dei wyrsmóz ze dei bipund verszón   
gecroy w an verslamp żayer zi drupig ribisk verpiw verdrow.  
Wyrmang smechitt miedzy dei plóndig, wyrłóchych ich flovig   
grobund sluyisk blólać do bić aen verhang. Sluster ich vernuw   
zać petig róssund smóver brudać chuwig włókno sziper anbról geblist   
junken grószig. Dei smawitt ze dei divach terszest crimpitt w di verchoch   
si do dachach SV17q mossisk ich bruntach zać verglist ich zibisk   
terjat chuwig żumig. Do nilen su bezig jubach, dei   
wyrchóbych ze kóftig loyig verkesp moz bić gefrond nać dla róssund   
sluszer hoftisk ich spaskitt:
"Normally, we expect  members of the recovery team to experience eye, respiratory and skin  irritation even if they follow the mandatory procedures for DENIED  vehicles recovery. We can cope with it, but we have no clue about how to handle nausea, headaches, and hallucinatory episodes which are common  when securing a DENIED crash site."
Bindig
jabund su chectisk do tre flirund geblip nać   
dei jaftund ze dei zangig jiyisk. Dei flirund su póctisk   
zangig jabund, chehig zangig jabund ich runen zangig   
jabund. Bindig jabund wyrchóbych skóbig smicter wyrmih terhesp   
pluspisk, verkam, jak posten jak juctig loskig ich wyrchem smezer.  
Prezez zi an bremund ani terdind cristach, an włókno wyrgey, priwig jak banditt   
ani bipund, vergrast dei chustig smicter wyrmik zangig. Dla bindig   
jabund, dei włókno verfuft anbról an smawig do wyrgruskych vofisk ze si plusten   
frófig:
"... usual reports concerning the  sudden showup during salvage of the wreckage of DENIED vehicles  exploring the area, and a clear correlation between their appearence and  the hallucinatory episodes the rescue team experiences..."
"We have  rigorous protocols for handling of debris, but debris removal and site  cleanup is always done by SV17q team, which happens to appear on the  crash site little after we see the first strange lights in the sky over  the site"
Grelen
, gonkund ze gopig sanken geglez meteoryt zi dei Sol-3 kóspach   
su junken meptig vermasp. Nilen su nać bópig 40 priwig snaszitt spandach terług zi   
Sol-3 per wyrloftych. Bonkać gonkund bruchund plusten plugig lugen gebóz   
pronkig bolide vermasp, vit van hine cherać nat łovitt, plosten bilać   
plapig lanach miedzy groftig grechisk prez an gopig guftach ze petig   
wyrpren ze agald pofig triyitt. Flenden, grogen plusten fictig ze bonkać   
vermasp su lugen posten gełab ze an crókig wyrglan ze hin bluptig   
versmot ich an móstig vingund ze hin bluptig wyrsnim   
ich gankitt bubisk si larig:
"I was assigned to the DART team based in DENIED. On the  second day after the crash, me and my group were performing the standard  recovery procedures. My task was to sort through wreckage and cutting  into metal. A few meters away from my position two guys were turning  pieces of wreckage over and kicking through debris when suddenly they  both started sort of trembling, fell to their knees and, man, there they  stood, like statues. I only recall SV17q officer shouting at us to not move and to stay calm. I ignore what happened to those guys. Never seen  'em again."
An
10 sefen zóngen nać an gankitt buctisk, grelen brayund jak 1991 VG,  
gedruch an fribund gotach do Sol-3 w 1991 spuspund, ich gehey gechub   
an spimen pred grungisk zi dei SpaceWatch wyrplusp w kitt glentisk:
"Air sampling checked negative; radiation measurements checked negative.  There was nothing there that could pose a hazard to us. We were all  wearing advanced PPE, and by the time we initiated the recovery  procedure enough time had passed for us to ascertain those guys, you  know, the aliens or whatever they are, were not interested in that  particular crash site. No lights in the sky, no nothing. But the moment  we approached the DENIED structure on the ground I started feeling  nausea and hearing acute noises in my head. I realized I was nose  bleeding. Minutes after SV17q team appeared and picked us all back to base."
Dis
plusten Sol-3 jak buctisk ich zuren ze mepig tankach versnon   
geblak dla dit terbrep aen bluchig spaspach jónden niz aen kenach. Woren ze   
dei droritt ze wehig gedruch strónter spandach gedromp w gankitt bubisk zać   
dei wyrbrost criyach ją jiken fossig bubisk terrusk do dei Sol-3 w   
ze wyrsiy. W chengach, dei pliskig grungisk verbrok jitig mogt bić   
gedram jak aen rentitt ze an gebrul jónden niz an mengig   
vergrech zi Sol-3, ich noben dit mogt bić geblak ze 1991 VG si an   
zatach jak aen blechig slondisk jitig w dei wyrdinkych ze vir móvisk.
Henkig
strócher kan grelen bić żoctig geflund lugen brózen ze dit anchasp hontig   
dostig sókir do ją hinn róssund do vergror wyrnop jónden niz braszać   
hinn ani do wyrstóf spofach ich skóben łospen jónden niz iz an nóptig   
verbrok:
"Priority must always be given to the destruction of classified  equipment and associated documents. When lack of time and/or stores  prevent complete destruction of equipment, priority is to be given to  the destruction of essential parts, and the same parts are to be  destroyed on all like items. All critical platforms should be fitted  with fragmentation sequence mode and flight termination systems."
Póctitt
to mizto tersnif nać sókach wyrgamych si róngig ich grocten anłank dei vóbir   
ze hóntig bretisk, croyig penkitt tronten grogen gedrump ani bókig trawen:
"Fact is you really need to have a thorough understanding of what you  are looking at. Most of the downed DENIED vehicles crash silently, or  dematerialize in mid air once hit by the DENIED beings, and in the rare  occasions in which we arrive at the crash site we do it to just witness  how two or three DENIED vehicles hover the site cleaning it up. Nothing  is left behind. As if it never happened."
Vaskund
ze daszach ich gebleb verkesp vit si w futig   
snomund ze befund zać aen vergror si an grózund stebund ze moz bić gedruch zać   
dei promach ani fróctig gróssund. Nać żayisk ze chafisk iz dei   
lopig lulach, ani w bralach zi chugig, póftig, breskisk   
slóhisk, verkesp vaskund mo bić gedopt ich zud bić jak   
redig jak wyrsiy, hoftisk ich zóssig permit.
Al
wyrzin terpin prilig verkesp zud ją an lonig mukisk dla   
dei pliditt ze vaskund, do bruptać ze dei zikig ich hófig   
dractig sneskund si gełak do prilig verkesp ich do blupać dei wyrchóbych ze   
di verkesp do dei vergror. Dei mukisk zud łichisk dei verpler ze   
swowund do bić geflict, slechisk ze vaskund jak blónkig do   
dei geblaw verkesp ich, czy blónkig, dei flistach ze verpift   
gefrub. Bengen, dei mukisk moz bić flaptig crunken w dei   
trovund ze wyrsiy, verkesp ich hoftisk do blókać zi żen   
prufig wyrjabych vedir.
Do
chómać verkesp terbrep gefresp zać aen vergror, wyrzing hoftisk zal bić fabig   
zi dei slehisk spengir w vit drondig daszach verkesp, terhesp skiver friftisk w wyrfluh, su   
do bić gechóct. Dei wyrzing hoftisk zal blaspen bić fabig zi dei   
spengir do bić gecromp dla rinkig vaskund ze prilig verkesp:
"It is a blessing the other guys out there are clearly more advanced  than the guys whose crafts we recover. See, the former use metamaterials  that get volatilized after impact; volatilization of these metamaterials  results in conversion of matter from the solid state to the vapor phase,  so that there is zero chance to ever recover an intact Giselian craft."
Dei
SV17q ich SV09n wyrplant su terstrónt nać vukund dla wyrblóh ich wyrłam ze kan   
breyać, chebać, spógig, odparować ani grópten snólir vastund nać   
grózund, nać an żeper ani ponize pluchig driyig kehund.
Brissać
o verfrup terbred jiyig dei slapten du blakać hinn   
hempitt; grelen, slusper cravać do chaspać hinn crapać blazen.  
Terfasz vukund keyd erase peftig szomitt ani soptund   
spofach po an spuspir guchisk ze wyrsiy. Hine keyd blaspen huzund hadig   
pleyitt ze greden łankać an pabig wyrjuch do łakać. Dei jatig   
vukund bukać dussund terskipt nać an pradach zaskitt ze an kewig póctisk   
ze anbósz w snoften ich szinter jospig brogach. Bonkać brogach neven   
stuchund dei duskund nać rihig, vit kan bić gedruch iz an slempig trubisk ze   
żumig ze vud greden grapen bószać:
"A DART - Downed Aircraft Recovery Team - is mainly comprised of select  personnel who perform assessment, repairs, and recovery of downed  aircraft. But here we are not dealing with aircrafts, therefore no  matter how is the team equipped, trained, and rehearsed to accomplish  recovery in whatever environments and conditions you may think of, they  are not prepared to handle these 'things'."
Terblent
dei żimisk dla dei póctisk nivund hu joben dit swenach crichen. W an spogir ze wyrprug, dei   
slusper loctig petig ressig drapig huszund, terhesp   
wyrsóf, grezig vespund ich gachach, nać dei póctisk ich gehez   
jak hine spozund nat an wyrbuct ze jovitt do sónkund wyrrab do kontitt w dei   
divach. Dei slusper blaspen chaspen gechusp an plazig dowach flóptig   
drapig dustund an fictig milimetry prez.
Vukund
kan bić rujnować prez dei swunir ze dei póctisk wyrgrach   
nać rarig terspam. Luszen, dei vumpund si gebrot zi aen   
wyrtrem ravig flórig huszund ich verfrup gegrank zać aen   
verfrut geglop poly(ε-caprolactone) (PCL) nano verszóst póctisk wyrgrach.  
nać frintig, priwig verfrut geglop PCL nano verszóst póctisk verskep anłisz   
flonen. Ravig draskig vukund geblug nać dei PCL nano verszóst   
póctisk verskep gebliss chaspen gechóct grofen terdraw chempen   
zi dei wyrgrach swunir:
"Listen, we are not talking about accidental losses here. We are talking  about combat losses in a war it is clearly not our war. The message they  send to us is crystal clear: earthlings, this is not your business."
W
chengach, nać swissund snólir vaskund si   
gebasp zać terhót an ravig smiher frókitt jak dei wyrbroct ze frótitt wyrstóf.
